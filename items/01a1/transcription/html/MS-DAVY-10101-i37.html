Solfatara Caldera

Solfatara di Pozzuoli is a volcanic crater near Naples. Though a dormant volcano, it emits jets of steam and sulphurous fumes.

Tufa

A variety of limestone formed when carbonate minerals precipitate out of water at ambient temperature.

✕

Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.

✕

Alum

Symbol/formula: xAl(SO4)2·12 H

A hydrated double sulphate salt of aluminium and another element such as potassium (potassium alum, or simply alum, KAl(SO4)2·12 H) or sodium (sodium alum, NaAl(SO4)2·12 H).

✕

Muriate of ammonia

Symbol/formula: NH4Cl

An inorganic compound and a white crystalline salt that is highly soluble in water. Solutions of ammonium chloride are mildly acidic. In its naturally occurring mineralogic form, it is known as sal ammoniac.
