Pumice

a type of extrusive volcanic rock, produced when lava with a very high content of water and gases is discharged from a volcano.

Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.
