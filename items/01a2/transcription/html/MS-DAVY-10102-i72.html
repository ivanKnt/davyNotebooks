|_Science for its progression
requires patronage; but it
must be a patronage
bestowed, a patronage
received with dignity.-
- It must be preserved
independent & free. It
can bear no fetters
not even golden fetters of gold,
& least of all those
fetters in which ignorance
or selfishness may attempt
to impose upon it
to shackle it.
- It wants only | thrives by liberal & | popular
protection.- It is a plant
