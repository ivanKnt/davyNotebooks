Nitrogene

Symbol/formula: N

Now known as nitrogen. A colourless and odourless gas, nitrogen is non-combustible and non-toxic. Davy also refers to this as azote.

✕

Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Aqua fortis

Archaic term for nitric acid.

✕

DanielRutherford

Scottish physician, chemist, and botanist who isolated nitrogen in 1772.

✕

Carl WilhelmScheele

Swedish-German pharmaceutical chemist. One of the discoverers of what would later be called oxygen (1771) as well as other chemical elements including what Davy renamed chlorine.
