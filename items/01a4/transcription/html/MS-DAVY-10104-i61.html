aeriform

Meaning having the form or nature of air; gaseous.

✕

the French Chemists

Possibly Lavoisier, Fourcroy, Berthollet and Macquer.

✕

Euchlorine

An archaic term describing a yellow or greenish-yellow gas, supposed to consist of chlorine dioxide with some free chlorine.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
