Mucilage

Mucilage is a thick gluey substance produced by nearly all plants and some microorganisms.

✕

Tannin

Biiter and astringent compounds found in plants. They include Ellagic acid, gallic acid, and pyrogallic acid which were first discovered by Braconnot in 1831.

✕

Alburnum

sapwood; the soft, newer wood in the trunk of a tree found between the bark and the hardened heartwood.

Gallic acid

Symbol/formula: C7H6O5
