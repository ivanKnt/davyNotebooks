Mimosa

Davy is referring to Acacia trees, particularly Acacia catechu, which are no longer classified as belonging to the mimosa genus.

✕

Gum Arabic

a natural gum originally consisting of the hardened sap of two species of the Acacia tree, Senegalia senegal and Vachellia seyal.

✕

Bengal Cutch

Also known as catechu. An extract of acacia trees used variously as a food additive, astringent, tannin, and dye.
