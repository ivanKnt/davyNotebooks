Scottish chemist and mineralogist whose writings contributed to the early spread of Dalton's atomic theory. FRSL.

Flint

Occasionally flintstone. A sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral quartz, categorised as the variety of chert that occurs in chalk or marly limestone.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.

Mucilage

Mucilage is a thick gluey substance produced by nearly all plants and some microorganisms.
