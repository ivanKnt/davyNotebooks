This lecture, an introduction to the chemical phenomena of nature, was delivered on Saturday 31st January, 1807. It is published in The Collected Works of Sir Humphry Davy, ed. John Davy, 9 vols. (Smith, Elder, London, 1839-1840), Vol 8, pp. 167-179. The manuscript is written in Davy's hand.
