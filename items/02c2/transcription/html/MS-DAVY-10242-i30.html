-: And Air rarified by Heat
in these districts is continually
ascending & giving place to
it -
-: The Up Strata of Air
are of a lower temperature
the higher we ascend -; &
hence a continued source exists
for the refrigeration of the lower
parts of the Atmosphere heated
by the transmission & communication
of Heat from the opaque
surface of the Earth -
-: The same principles will
apply to the equalization
of the temperature of water -
- Rivers rising in high grounds
bring a cool & salubrious
fluid into the lowlands
- Springs are colder than
the surface in the summer
