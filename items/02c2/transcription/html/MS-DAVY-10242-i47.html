Frederick WilliamHerschel

Hanoverian astronomer and composer who while living in Bath discovered the planet later named Uranus. Moving to Slough he built the world's largest telescope to date which Davy visited when he was courting Jane Apreece. FRSL.

IsaacNewton

Prominent English natural philosopher and mathematician greatly admired by Davy who once under the influence of several litres of nitrous oxide wrote in his notebook 'Davy & Newton' in one inch letters. FRSL.
