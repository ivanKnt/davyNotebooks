Elastic fluid

A descriptive term for a gas, emphasising its elastic quality of spontaneous expansion.

✕

Atmospheric air

The air which is prevailing under atmospheric conditions. Earth's atmosphere is composed of about 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and 1% percent other gases.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Carbonic acid

Symbol/formula: CO2 or H2CO3

Now known as Carbon dioxide (CO2), or a solution of carbon dioxide in water (H2CO3).

✕

Mucilage

Mucilage is a thick gluey substance produced by nearly all plants and some microorganisms.
