- Vegetable like animal life
has its limits fixed & definite -
The cedar the being of a thousand years
at length yields to decay - as well
as the fungus that exists only for as
many hours - The common law
must be obeyed. - Generation
succeeds to generation & the grand
features only are preserved - Art
may improve - but cannot render
immutable - & immortality is equally
denied to all the productions of the
Earth. -
