Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.

✕

Vit electricity

Vitreous electricity', produced by rubbing glass or gems.

✕

Res: electricity

Resinous electricity', obtained by rubbing amber, silk, or paper.
