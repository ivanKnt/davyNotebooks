Tin

Symbol/formula: Sn

A silvery-coloured metal, tin is soft enough to be cut with little force, and a bar of tin can be bent by hand with little effort.

✕

Bismuth

Symbol/formula: Bi

A high-density, silvery, pink-tinged metal.

✕

Ammonia of copper

Symbol/formula: Cu(OH)2

Copper(II) ion reacts with stoichiometric quantities of aqueous ammonia to precipitate light blue Cu(OH)2. Some basic salts may also form.

✕

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

Wolfram

Symbol/formula: W

Also known as tungsten. Tungsten is an exceptionally strong refractory metal. It was identified as a new element in 1781 and first isolated as a metal in 1783.
