:I shall have to offer to You various
new experiments & new illustrations.-
You will I trust find that in
the last year even, some progress
has been made towards a more
complete arrangement of
the facts, & that Science
has neither slept, nor been
slow in its progress- The
approbation which you so kindly
bestowed upon early & imperfect
labours has not failed to
be a stimulus to what
I hope are better exertions
& I trust it will be found
that any encouragement offered
to experimental Science will
never be inefficacious or
vain.-
