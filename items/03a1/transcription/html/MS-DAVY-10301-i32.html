EdwardNairne

Possibly the most well known scientific instrument maker of his time. Worked closely with Franklin, Priestley and Cavendish. 'Nairne's Machine' referred to his patented electrostatic generator, which was intended to treat a variety of medicinal ailments via electrical shocks. FRSL.

Leyden Jar

The Leyden Jar is an electrical component which stores a high-voltage electric charge between electrical conductors on the inside and outside of a glass jar.
