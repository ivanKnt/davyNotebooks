Lecture III. 1809. Jany 2d
Oxygene
Amongst the great classes of
Natural objects none are
more calculated to arrest
the attention of the inquisitive
mind than those belonging
to the atmosphere.
- The air is the medium
by which light & heat are
transmitted to us from the
sun. It is the seat of clouds
the birthplace of winds & the
source of rain & dew of
rivers & springs. Vegetable
& animal life - Vegetables
& animals are supported
by it [&] life is connected.
