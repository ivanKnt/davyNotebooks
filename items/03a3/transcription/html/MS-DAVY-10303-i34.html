Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

✕

Porphyry

A hard, igneous purplish-red rock in which crystals of feldspar are embedded in a fine-grained groundmass.

✕

Feldspar

An abundant rock-forming mineral typically occurring as colourless or pale-coloured crystals and consisting of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium.

✕

Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

✕

Limestone

A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3).
