Arabian Peninsula

a peninsula in West Asia, situated northeast of Africa on the Arabian Plate. It includes the territories of includes Bahrain, Kuwait, Oman, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, the United Arab Emirates and Yemen, as well as southern Iraq and Jordan. In the classical era, the Sinai Peninsula was also considered a part of Arabia.
