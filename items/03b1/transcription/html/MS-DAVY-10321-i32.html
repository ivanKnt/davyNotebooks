StephenHales

English clergyman who made major contributions to a range of scientific fields including botany, pneumatic chemistry and physiology. FRSL.

JosephBlack

From 1766, Professor of Medcine and Chemistry at the University of Edinburgh. Discovered latent heat and fixed air, later renamed carbon dioxide. A correspondent of James Watt and Thomas Beddoes (who studied under him).

Lime

Symbol/formula: CaO

Commonly known as quicklime or burnt lime, calcium oxide is a widely used chemical compound. It is a white, caustic, alkaline, crystalline solid at room temperature.

Magnesia

Symbol/formula: MgO

A white hygroscopic solid mineral that occurs naturally as periclase and is a source of magnesium.

Elastic fluid
