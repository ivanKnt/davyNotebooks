WilliamGilbert

English physician, man of science, and author of the influential treatise De Magnete (1600).

Topaz

Symbol/formula: Al2SiO4(F,OH)2

Topaz is a silicate mineral of aluminium and fluorine.

Tourmaline

Tourmaline is a boron silicate. Opaque and black in colour, tourmaline is a semi-precious stone that can be found in a variety of colours based on the presence of iron, magnesium, or lithium. Boron was first isolated by Davy in 1808.
