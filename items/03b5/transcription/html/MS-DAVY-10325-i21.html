Muriate of soda

Symbol/formula: NaCl

Also known as sodium chloride, or common salt.

✕

Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.

✕

Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.

WilliamCruickshank

Professor of chemistry at the Royal Military Academy, Woolwich, who invented the trough battery. FRSL.

✕

CharlesDesormes

Desormes was a French physicist and chemist. He did most of his scientific work in collaboration with his son-in-law, Nicolas Clément.

AntonioPacchioni

✕

RobertPeel

Conservative politician, serving as Home Secretary and Prime Minister. Knew Davy as a Royal Institution Annual Subscriber and also through the Royal Society of London where he helped Davy establish its Royal Medals and the Zoological Society. FRSL.

✕

WilliamNicholson

Founding editor of A Journal of Natural Philosophy, Chemistry, and the Arts (1797-1814) where Davy published some of his early papers. Part of the Godwin circle.
