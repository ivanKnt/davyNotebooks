Scheele the discoverer of Oxymuriatic
acid gas was conceived that
it was Muriatic acid deprived
of Hydrogene or as it Phlogiston
; but Mr Berthollet to whom
we owe its application asserted
that |muriatic acid| by its action upon Manganese
it became combined with Oxygene ;
& that muriatic acid gas
was an undecomposable
body & oxymuriatic acid gas
a body easily decompounded;
- And He attempted to prove
this by exposing oxymuriatic
acid dissolved in water to
light oxymuriatic acid | Oxygene gas| is
evolved & common Muriatic
acid is found dissolved
in the water.-

Oxymuriatic acid

Symbol/formula: Cl

Chlorine. Chlorine is a yellow-green gas at room temperature, with a pungent, irritating odour that is detectable at low concentrations.

✕

Muriatic acid

Symbol/formula: HCl

Hydrochloric acid. Davy also refers to this as muriatic gas and marine acid.

✕

Hydrogene

Symbol/formula: H

Now known as hydrogen. Hydrogen is the lightest element and, at standard conditions, is a gas of diatomic molecules. It is colourless, odourless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible.

✕

Phlogiston

A hypothetical substance, thought of as an elastic fluid, which was believed to exist in combination in all combustible bodies.

✕

Manganese

Symbol/formula: Mn

A hard, brittle, silvery metal, often found in minerals in combination with iron. Manganese was first isolated in the 1770s.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
