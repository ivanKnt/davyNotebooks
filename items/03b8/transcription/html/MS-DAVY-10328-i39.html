- The Natives of New Holland
who perhaps are the lowest of human
beings in the scale of Civilization,
possess the mode of inflaming wood

point of a |cyclindrical| piece of wood, & they
move this round as rapidly as possible
in their hands, till it begins to
smoke, dry leaves are strewed
