The first impressions received from
the exterior of the Globe are those of
diversity, variety & beauty. - -
Hills, Valiles & Plains appear covered
with different soils & affording
different vegetables. Mountain
chains are seen presenting irregular
summits, bare or capped with
snow, or emitting volcanic fires,
& their foundations form, the beds
of rivers, or the shores of Lakes,
Seas & the Ocean. - -
