proofs of the great alterations
that the Globe has undergone. --
The most sublime speculations are
awakened. - the mind is carried
into past ages. - New forms of

& a boundless enquiry. - the
destruction of a former order of things
& a system arranged with harmony,
filled with beauty & life, - formed
from its elements, & established
