-A supposed new Class of Rocks is
introduced under the name of
Transition Rocks, conceived to differ
from the Crystallized primary Rocks,
& from the Rocks containing
Organic remains. This distinction
has been little adopted. The
Shades of difference in nature
are undoubtedly wonderfully various,
but it is dangerous to attempt to
connect them, in particular cases
only, with general Classification;
for each order should be marked
& distinct, & possess undeviating
Characters. -
