- In an appendix to the Abbe Hauy 's
System of Minerals - there is likewise a very
philosophical view of the Nature &
Classes of the principal Rocks & Strata.
The descriptions are peculiarly accurate,
& expressed with peculiar felicity.-
The only objection to the Work is, that
newly invented terms are in all cases
adopted, but the Author has likewise
fortunately affixed the established &
common Names. - I trust that
these will still be used, |_And that
in this Country at least, no love of
innovation will induce us, a second
time, to embrace a new, & imperfect
Nomenclature from the French.-
|Philosophical| Truth. as I have said upon
a former occasion, has |can have| no relation
to National feelings. I have the |venerate|
highest respect for the Abbe Hauy
