Strata, parallel or very slightly

The first consist for the most part
of Chrystalline stony matter, they
contain few fragments; and exhibit
few vestiges of a former order of things. -
These are called Primary Rocks. -
- - The second order usually contains
abundance of Mechanical deposited,
water worn stones, sand, & even clay
& they often abound in vegetable
remains, & the exuviae of marine
animals, These are called
the secondary Rocks. - - -
