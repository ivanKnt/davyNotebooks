Neptunian Theory

A theory of geology proposed by Abraham Gottlob Werner, who proposed that rocks formed from the crystallisation of minerals in the early Earth's oceans.

✕

Plutonic Theory

Relates to the Plutonist theories of James Hutton (1715-1795), who proposed that the earth's igneous rocks were formed from magma.
