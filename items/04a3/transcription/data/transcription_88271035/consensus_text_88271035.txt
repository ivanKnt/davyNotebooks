In the operations of Nature,
there is a continued succession; a destruction,
& renovation of forms. - But our view,
& our period of existence, limit us
to the few objects surrounding us. -
Suns & Worlds may be created, &
may decay, by uniform Laws, ordained
by infinite Wisdom, & our Earth, as
a part of the Planetary System, may
participate in a [underline]New Order[/underline]. - But
these objects are far beyond Mortal Men;
the imagination soars towards them in
vain; its efforts are idle &
unavailing; its strength is wasted
in dreams. -
