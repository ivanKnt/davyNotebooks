Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

✕

Pearlspar

Symbol/formula: CaMg(CO₃)₂

An alternative name for dolomite. Dolomite is an anhydrous carbonate mineral composed of calcium magnesium carbonate.

✕

Witherite

Symbol/formula: BaCO3

a barium carbonate mineral in the aragonite group
