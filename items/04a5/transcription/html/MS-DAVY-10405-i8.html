Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

✕

Serpentine

A metamorphic rock containing one or more of the serpentine group minerals.

Lizard Point

The Lizard is a peninsula of the southern coast of Cornwall. It is the most southerly point of the British mainland. The geology of the Lizard is complex: it contains some of Cornwall's oldest (Precambrian) rocks, as well as heavily metamorphosed rocks.
