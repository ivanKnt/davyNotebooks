Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Petuntse

Also spelled petunse and bai dunzi, baidunzi. A historic term for a wide range of micaceous or feldspathic rocks suitable after processing as an ingredient in some ceramic formulations.

✕

Kaolin

Symbol/formula: Al₂Si₂O₅(OH)₄

Commonly called 'china clay', a hydrated aluminum silicate crystalline mineral formed over many millions of years by the hydrothermal decomposition of granite rocks.
