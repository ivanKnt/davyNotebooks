Fluorboric acid

Symbol/formula: H⁺[BF₄]⁻

Also called tetrafluoroboric acid (archaically, fluoboric acid), an inorganic compound. The term "fluoroboric acid" refers to a range of chemical compounds, depending on the solvent.

Ammonia

Symbol/formula: NH3

An inorganic chemical compound of nitrogen and hydrogen. Ammonia is a colourless gas with a distinct pungent smell.

Carbonate of potash

Symbol/formula: K2CO3

Potassium carbonate. It is a white salt, which is soluble in water and forms a strongly alkaline solution.

Mercury

Symbol/formula: Hg

Also known as quicksilver. A heavy, silvery element, mercury is the only metallic element that is known to be liquid at standard temperature and pressure.
