Six grains of fusible substance
exposed to 31. cubical inches
of oxyl acid gas containing
1/6 of unabsorbable gas.
- The action took place
but without violence & with
very little [xxx] heat . .-: &
a great expansion of the
gas; from which it appears
that oxy muriatic acid gas is
formed. - & Muriate of
potash but the retort
became loose so that the

Muriatic acid

Symbol/formula: HCl

Hydrochloric acid. Davy also refers to this as muriatic gas and marine acid.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.
