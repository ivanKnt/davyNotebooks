Silver nitrate

Symbol/formula: AgNO3

Silver is dissolved in nitric acid and this solution is then evaporated to leave a white crystalline solid.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.

✕

Ethylene

Symbol/formula: C2H4

Ethylene is a flammable hydrocarbon gas.

✕

D°

Davy's shorthand for 'ditto' (meaning, the same thing again; a repetition of something; an indication of agreeing with something already said so as to avoid repetition).
