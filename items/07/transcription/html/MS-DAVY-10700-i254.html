✕

Oxymuriatic acid

Symbol/formula: Cl

Chlorine. Chlorine is a yellow-green gas at room temperature, with a pungent, irritating odour that is detectable at low concentrations.

✕

Nitrogene

Symbol/formula: N

Now known as nitrogen. A colourless and odourless gas, nitrogen is non-combustible and non-toxic. Davy also refers to this as azote.

✕

Ox acid

Symbol/formula: Cl

Now known as chlorine. Chlorine is a yellow-green gas at room temperature, with a pungent, irritating odour that is detectable at low concentrations.

✕

D°

Davy's shorthand for 'ditto' (meaning, the same thing again; a repetition of something; an indication of agreeing with something already said so as to avoid repetition).
