Camphor

Symbol/formula: C10H16O

A chemical made by distilling the wood and bark of the camphor tree. Commonly used in lotions and creams to relieve pain and irritation

✕

Muriatic acid

Symbol/formula: HCl

Hydrochloric acid. Davy also refers to this as muriatic gas and marine acid.

✕

Oxymuriatic acid

Symbol/formula: Cl

Chlorine. Chlorine is a yellow-green gas at room temperature, with a pungent, irritating odour that is detectable at low concentrations.
