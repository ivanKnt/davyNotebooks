Boric acid

Symbol/formula: B(OH)3

A compound of boron, oxygen, and hydrogen. Davy often refers to this as boracic acid.

✕

Red lead

Lead (II,IV) oxide. A bright red or solid orange, used in pigment, the manufacture of batteries, and rust-proof primer paints.

✕

Platina

An alloy of platinum and several other metals, including palladium, osmium, and iridium.

✕

Potassium

Symbol/formula: K

A silvery white metal that is soft enough to easily cut with a knife. Potassium metal reacts rapidly with atmospheric oxygen to form flaky white potassium peroxide in only seconds of exposure.

✕

Oxymuriatic gas

Symbol/formula: Cl

Chlorine gas.
