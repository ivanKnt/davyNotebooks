Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Nitre

Symbol/formula: KNO3

Potassium nitrate. The mineral form is saltpetre, which was useful as a fertiliser and for making gunpowder.

✕

Nitrogene

Symbol/formula: N

Now known as nitrogen. A colourless and odourless gas, nitrogen is non-combustible and non-toxic. Davy also refers to this as azote.

✕

Platina

An alloy of platinum and several other metals, including palladium, osmium, and iridium.

✕

Boric acid

Symbol/formula: B(OH)3

A compound of boron, oxygen, and hydrogen. Davy often refers to this as boracic acid.
