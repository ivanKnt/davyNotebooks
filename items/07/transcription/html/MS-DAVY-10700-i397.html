Ammonia

Symbol/formula: NH3

An inorganic chemical compound of nitrogen and hydrogen. Ammonia is a colourless gas with a distinct pungent smell.

✕

Nitrous oxide

Symbol/formula: N2O

Later known as 'laughing gas'. Davy discovered that nitrous oxide was respirable when not contaminated with nitric oxide (NO). Davy researched into the physiological effects of nitrous oxide.

✕

Common air

The air of the lower stratum of the atmosphere, or the air that we breathe, which is a mixture of several gases, including oxygen (O2), nitrogen (N2) and carbon dioxide (CO2).

✕

Silver nitrate

Symbol/formula: AgNO3

Silver is dissolved in nitric acid and this solution is then evaporated to leave a white crystalline solid.

✕

Baryte

Symbol/formula: BaSO4

A mineral consisting of barium sulphate.

retort

In a chemistry laboratory, a retort is a device used for distillation or dry distillation of substances.
