Muriate of silver

Symbol/formula: AgCl

Silver chloride. A white crystalline solid well-known for its low solubility in water and its sensitivity to light.

✕

Mercury

Symbol/formula: Hg

Also known as quicksilver. A heavy, silvery element, mercury is the only metallic element that is known to be liquid at standard temperature and pressure.

✕

Oxide of mercury

Symbol/formula: HgO

An inorganic compound, mercury oxide is red or orange in colour, and a solid at room temperature and pressure. The mineral form montroydite is very rarely found.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
