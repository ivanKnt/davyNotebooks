WilliamPayne

Davy's laboratory assistant at the Royal Institution 1803-1813. His dismissal, for fighting, paved the way for Faraday's appointment in his place. His dates of birth and death are not known.

Bateman's Buildings, Soho Square

Bateman's Buildings formed part of the connection between Soho Square and Bateman Street in London. This may have been an address for Davy's laboratory assistant William Payne.
