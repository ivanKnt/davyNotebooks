1819
March. 3.
Put into the furnace the Iron button of Feby
23 with some more from greenwood & finely divided
clay _ when taken out had fuzed into an
irregular button which quenched in water was
found to be Steel _ annealed it for 2 hours.
(See March 5)
Put in at the same time 91.8 grain of
Platinum in small pieces and 10 grains of best
Steel also in small pieces - when taken out
found it agglutinated but the Platinum had
not run down(See March 5 th )
- 5.th
Put in the [xxx] Iron button of the third with more
Iron and green wood but the crucible gave way from
the heat sank down burst & the metal was lost
(crucible specimen)
Put in also the Platinum button of the third
3 rd with 10 gr more of Steel - It ran into a
globule which had been nearly in perfect fusion
(See the March 8th)
