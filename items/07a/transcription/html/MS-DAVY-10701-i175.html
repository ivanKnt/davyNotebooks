Platinum

Symbol/formula: Pt

A dense, malleable, ductile, highly unreactive, precious, silverish-white transition metal.

Steel

Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon with improved strength and fracture resistance compared to other forms of iron.

Nickel

Symbol/formula: Ni

A silvery-white lustrous metal with a slight golden tinge. Nickel is a hard and ductile transition metal.

✕

Charcoal

A lightweight black carbon residue produced by strongly heating wood (or other animal and plant materials) in minimal oxygen to remove all water and volatile constituents.

✕

Iron oxide

Symbol/formula: Fe2O3

Oxide of iron. A dark red solid which occurs as the mineral haematite and as the main component of rust.

Silex

Symbol/formula: SiO2

Silica, an oxide of silicon.

Alumine

Symbol/formula: AL2O3

Alumine is an archaic name for alumina, which is aluminium oxide.

Lime

Symbol/formula: CaO

Commonly known as quicklime or burnt lime, calcium oxide is a widely used chemical compound. It is a white, caustic, alkaline, crystalline solid at room temperature.

A mineral consisting of barium sulphate.
