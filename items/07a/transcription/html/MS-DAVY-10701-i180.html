George O'BrienWyndham

From 1763 3rd Earl of Egremont. Very wealthy collector and agriculturalist who criticised some of Davy's work. FRSL.

✕

Lime

Symbol/formula: CaO

Commonly known as quicklime or burnt lime, calcium oxide is a widely used chemical compound. It is a white, caustic, alkaline, crystalline solid at room temperature.

✕

Soda

An alkaline substance obtained from the ashes of marine or salt-impregnated plants, especially species of Salsola. Davy decomposed soda to reveal its elemental sodium.

✕

Alkalimeter

Invented by François-Antoine-Henri Descroizilles in 1791, this was first used to measure the quantity of chlorine in dyes and it is the first use of volumetric titration in chemistry.
