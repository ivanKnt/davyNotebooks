Voltaic

Cream of lime

A scum of calcium carbonate formed on a solution of milk of lime by combination with the carbon dioxide of the air and a a mixture of slaked lime and water.

Ammonia

Symbol/formula: NH3

An inorganic chemical compound of nitrogen and hydrogen. Ammonia is a colourless gas with a distinct pungent smell.
