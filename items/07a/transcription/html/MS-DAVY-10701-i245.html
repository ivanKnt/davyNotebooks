Nitre

Symbol/formula: KNO3

Potassium nitrate. The mineral form is saltpetre, which was useful as a fertiliser and for making gunpowder.

✕

Soda

An alkaline substance obtained from the ashes of marine or salt-impregnated plants, especially species of Salsola. Davy decomposed soda to reveal its elemental sodium.

✕

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

✕

Platinum

Symbol/formula: Pt

A dense, malleable, ductile, highly unreactive, precious, silverish-white transition metal.

✕

D°

Davy's shorthand for 'ditto' (meaning, the same thing again; a repetition of something; an indication of agreeing with something already said so as to avoid repetition).
