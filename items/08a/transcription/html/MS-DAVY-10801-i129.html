Cream of lime

A scum of calcium carbonate formed on a solution of milk of lime by combination with the carbon dioxide of the air and a a mixture of slaked lime and water.

Lime

Symbol/formula: CaO

Commonly known as quicklime or burnt lime, calcium oxide is a widely used chemical compound. It is a white, caustic, alkaline, crystalline solid at room temperature.
