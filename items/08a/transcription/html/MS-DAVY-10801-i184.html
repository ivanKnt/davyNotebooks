Oil of vitriol

Symbol/formula: H2SO4

Sulphuric acid. A mineral acid composed of the elements sulphur, oxygen, and hydrogen.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.

✕

Napthalene

Symbol/formula: C10H8

The simplest polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon. A white crystalline solid with a characteristic odour.

✕

Camphor

Symbol/formula: C10H16O

A chemical made by distilling the wood and bark of the camphor tree. Commonly used in lotions and creams to relieve pain and irritation

Silk
