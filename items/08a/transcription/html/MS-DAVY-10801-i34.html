Muriate of tin

Symbol/formula: SnCl4

Stannic chloride. Tin chloride is a white crystalline solid. It forms a stable dihydrate, but aqueous solutions tend to undergo hydrolysis, particularly if hot.

✕

Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.

✕

Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.
