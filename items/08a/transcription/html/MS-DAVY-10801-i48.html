1822 Aug 21. Salts of Uranium (various) precipitated by ammo-
nia and left to dry in the air became Carbon a yellow pulve-
rulent body dissolving in acids (nitric & Mur.) with the evolution
of Carbonic Acid - heated highly the Carbonic Acid was driven off
the powder aggregated - contracting in dimension - and formed
a hard dark coloured purplish lump. it dissolved in acids with more
difficulty than before but without the liberation of oxygen or any
gas the state of oxide appears unchanged - powdered in agate it formed
a yellowish powder.
