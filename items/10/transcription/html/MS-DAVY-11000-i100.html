William ThomasBrande

Originally trained as an apothecary, Brande became a chemist. With Davy and others founded the Animal Chemistry Club. Lectured on chemistry in various London institutions and, following Davy's retirement succeeded him in his positions at the Royal Institution where he founded The Quarterly Journal of Science. A Secretary of the Royal Society of London, 1816-1826; later fell out with Davy. FRSL.
