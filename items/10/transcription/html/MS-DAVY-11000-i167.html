Hydrogene

Symbol/formula: H

Now known as hydrogen. Hydrogen is the lightest element and, at standard conditions, is a gas of diatomic molecules. It is colourless, odourless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible.

Atmospheric air

The air which is prevailing under atmospheric conditions. Earth's atmosphere is composed of about 78% nitrogen, 21% oxygen, and 1% percent other gases.
