Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.

✕

Corrosive sublimate

Symbol/formula: HgCl2

Also known as mercury dichloride, it is a white crystalline solid and a molecular compound that is very toxic to humans.

✕

Silver nitrate

Symbol/formula: AgNO3

Silver is dissolved in nitric acid and this solution is then evaporated to leave a white crystalline solid.

Chlorine

Symbol/formula: Cl

Davy named chlorine in 1810 and insisted it was an element and not a compound.

MichaelFaraday

From 1812 Davy used Faraday as his amanuensis and the following year secured his appointment as laboratory assistant at the Royal Institution. Despite a difficult relationship with Davy, Faraday later became the dominent figure at the Royal Institution and one of the leading scientific personalities of the middle third of the nineteenth century.
