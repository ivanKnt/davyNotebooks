Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.

✕

Muriatic acid

Symbol/formula: HCl

Hydrochloric acid. Davy also refers to this as muriatic gas and marine acid.

✕

Silver nitrate

Symbol/formula: AgNO3

Silver is dissolved in nitric acid and this solution is then evaporated to leave a white crystalline solid.

✕

Phosphorus acid

Symbol/formula: H3PO3

Different to phosphoric acid (H3PO4), phosphorus acid is an intermediate in the preparation of other phosphorus compounds.

Pierre LouisDulong

French physicist and chemist who worked mostly on specific heats, but also discovered nitrogen chloride around the same time as Davy. ForMemRSL.

✕

Jöns JacobBerzelius

Swedish chemist. He discovered the chemical elements cerium and selenium, and was the first to isolate silicon, thorium, titanium, and zirconium. A rival of Davy's whom he met a few times.
