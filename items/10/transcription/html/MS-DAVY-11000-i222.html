Chlorine

Symbol/formula: Cl

Davy named chlorine in 1810 and insisted it was an element and not a compound.

✕

Muriate of lime

Symbol/formula: CaCl2

An inorganic compound and a white crystalline solid at room temperature. Calcium chloride is highly soluble in water, and can be created by neutralising hydrochloric acid with calcium hydroxide.

✕

Phosphorus

Symbol/formula: P

A non-metallic chemical element of the nitrogen family. A colourless, semitransparent, soft, waxy solid.

✕

Corrosive sublimate

Symbol/formula: HgCl2

Also known as mercury dichloride, it is a white crystalline solid and a molecular compound that is very toxic to humans.
