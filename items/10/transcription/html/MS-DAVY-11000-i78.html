I wish for 25 extra copies
Dear Sir
The paper on these sheets is for the Journal
of the Royal Institution Will you have the goodness to
send it immediately to the press & be so good
as to give me a proof of it at the Post Office
Whitehaven . - You will be glad to hear that
my lamps are now every where in general use
in the northern collieries & the benefits arising
from them fully felt -
to Wm Bulmer Esqre
I am Dear Sir very
sincerely yours H Davy
