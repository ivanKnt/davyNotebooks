I was induced to turn my attention
to the subject. I went to the North
of England & visited |some of| the principal
collieries in the neighborhood of
Newcastle & conversed with some
of the most intelligent for the
purpose of ascertaining the state |condition| of
the collieries |workings| & of their ventilation.
- I found the greatest desire to
assist in particular |to assist my enquiries| both in the
coal owners, & in the Inspectors
or viewers of the mines & the
Gentlemen acquainted with the state | condition |
at the Northern collieries |as well as the Inspectors or viewers of the mines| & I have
particular obligations on this point
to Cuthbert Ellison Esqr MP, the Revd
John Hodgson, Dr Fenwick Dr Clanny
Mr Buddle Mr Dunn & Mr Fenwick.-
