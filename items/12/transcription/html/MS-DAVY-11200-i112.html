Ammonia

Symbol/formula: NH3

An inorganic chemical compound of nitrogen and hydrogen. Ammonia is a colourless gas with a distinct pungent smell.

✕

Platinum oxide

Symbol/formula: PtO2

Compound composed of the elements platinum and oxygen. It is a dark brown powder which becomes an active catalyst when exposed to hydrogen. Its highly insoluble and thermally stable properties make it suitable for glass and ceramic applications.

✕

Carbonate of lime

Symbol/formula: CaCO3

Calcium carbonate.

Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

frit

A word to describe the materials, either partly or fully fused by heating, from which glass is made.
