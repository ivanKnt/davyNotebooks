snipe

Medium sized, skulking wading birds with short legs and long, straight bills.

Quail

A type of small gamebird belonging to the same order as turkeys and chickens; often killed for sport and food.

Moor hen

Despite its name, the moorhen is a medium-sized water bird, found mainly in marshy or watery habitats.

Jackdaw

A small, black-plumed bird belonging to the corvid, or crow, family.

Woodcock

A large wading bird; its genus name Scolopax is Latin for a snipe.
