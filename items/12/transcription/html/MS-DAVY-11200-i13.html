Fluate of potassa

A fluate is a salt formed by the fluoric acid combined with a base; fluate of potassa is the combination of fluoric acid and potash.

Chlorine

Symbol/formula: Cl

Davy named chlorine in 1810 and insisted it was an element and not a compound.

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
