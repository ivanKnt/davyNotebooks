Banks of the Rhone , Jany 6
11. o,Clock
The air is soft as in the month of june
In northern climes, a balmy zepher |blows.|
And nothing speaks of winters harshest |month|
Save that the trees are leafless
And yon Alps encased in snows.
Capped by the snow &
girt around with ice
Not as in summer merely capped by |snows|
But deep encased & girt around by ice
