FrancescoPetrarch

An Italian poet and scholar, famous for his love sonnets. Davy wrote a poem called 'Vaucluse' inspired by Petrarch's poetry on the river Sorgue and Vaucluse valley in the Provence region of France and called him the 'bard of provence' in another poem, which John Davy published with the title 'Banks of the Rhone'.
