A piece of prepared skin 1/5 of
an inch thick
introduced into the strongest
infusion of [Et] [Cal]. was appeared
to be well tanned in
about 4 days – It was
however suffered to remain
in the infusion for about
a week – When it was
dried. After this time
it was dried – The leather
was well colored & was
approved of both as to
appearance & texture by
Mr Purkis & Mr Poole . –
