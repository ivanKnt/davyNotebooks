My dear Sir,
The little boy
who brings you this letter is
my brother – It is my desire
& it is my mothers desire
that He should become your
pupil. –
I fear his mind at
present is in a very
uncultivated state; but He
seems to possess sensibility:
which I have been accustomed
to consider as the foundation
of all power & activity. –
Under your tuition at
all events, He must be
improved & if He does is
