What once was beauty & form
Is mouldering dust. –
What once was strength & power
Is still beneath the turf –
Is there aught in your dying forms
That gave to my Spirit energy. –
Was the blood that rolled through
your veins. –
The infant germ of my power

that whiten it came
From the breath & the spirit
of man. –

Thoughts roll not beneath
the dust. –
No feeling is in the
cold grave. –
Neither thought nor feeling
can die –
They have passed to other
