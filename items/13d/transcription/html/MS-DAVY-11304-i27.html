5
Its absorption by Animals. –

The enthusiasm produced by discovery
no longer exists in my mind: In
calmly considering the effects produced
by the inspiration of the gaseous
oxyd of Azote on myself they
appear astonishing & important
as affording hopes of effecting
considerable changes in the
human constitution

But I was then unable to procure
it in quantities sufficient
to the subject to any extent.

✕

Nitrous oxide

Symbol/formula: N2O

Later known as 'laughing gas'. Davy discovered that nitrous oxide was respirable when not contaminated with nitric oxide (NO). Davy researched into the physiological effects of nitrous oxide.

✕

Nitrogene

Symbol/formula: N

Now known as nitrogen. A colourless and odourless gas, nitrogen is non-combustible and non-toxic. Davy also refers to this as azote.
