Man is a being Whose organization seems totally
fitted for the Display of Intellectual Powers. & in this His superiority
over the other Classes of organized beings seems Chiefly to Consist.
For the most perfect Brute is unable to display any of those.
acts of the Mind whereby Man gains the knowledge of Truth
