– On Happiness –
– Definitions –
1st. The Love of Pleasure & the Aversion to Pain are
Instinctive Principles impressed on the Human Mind
from the first Moment of its Existence
2 They are the Instigating Principles to Action in Man
3 The Different Modifications of these Principles
are called Passions –
4. Reason is the Regulating Principle by which
the Passions are governed –
– Postulates –
1. Grant the Existence of a supreme
Intelligent Agent
