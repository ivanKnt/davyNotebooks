The best mode of ascertaining
the Nature of the new
compound will be to throw
it into oil of turpentine
& then into Ether -
if it forms potash in
Ether: there may be
a doubt; but if it
is resolved into a
gas & does not
form potash; but forms
a gas producing potash
by combustion then it
is Alkaligen. -
