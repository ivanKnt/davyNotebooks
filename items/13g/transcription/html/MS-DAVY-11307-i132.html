Summers Heath blast; winters snows
Disturb not your supreme repose. ~
nor the mild vivifying breath of spring
Cloathing the lowlands, all in green |green|
Creating round a joyful scene
Aught of change to [grace:] can bring
[Hot] [Oer] | e'en| the purple heath expands
Its foliage oer your blanched sands;~
Your Rocks alone the yellow lichen covers
In tints unfading as their own
No shapes of life are known
Save where the Eagle Hovers. ~
