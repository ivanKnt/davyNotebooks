ThomasErastus

Thomas Erastus was a Swiss physician and Calvinist theologian. He published several pieces focused on medicine, astrology, alchemy, and attacked in his publications the system of Paracelsus. In doing so, he defended medieval tradition in general, and Galen in particular, while conceding some merit to specific points in Paracelsus.
