Thomas AndrewKnight

Knight was a botanist and horticulturalist. He was the second president of the Royal Horticulture Society. Davy frequently fished on Knight's esates in the Welsh Marches. FRSL.

Charles FrancisGreville

A British antiquarian, collector and politician who sat in the House of Commons from 1774 to 1790. FRSL.

Naphtha

A flammable liquid hydrocarbon mixture. Generally, it is a fraction of crude oil, but it can also be produced from natural gas condensates, petroleum distillates, and the fractional distillation of coal tar and peat.
