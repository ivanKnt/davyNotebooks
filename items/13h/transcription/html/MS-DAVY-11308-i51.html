Theora

Davy returns to the character of the legendary ''Theora'' a woman who died in when a ship that came from Ireland wrecked off the coast of Cornwall in several poems (Wahida Amin, 57, 67), including as a fictional character in Davy's narrative about the legendry Brutus of Troy.
