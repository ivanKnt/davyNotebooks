23
Exhaustless as the unfathomed sea -
That to the her lover gives
That vital dew which each that lives:
Absorbs - demands with joy inhales
Whether from the peaceful gales -
Breathing oer the happy isles.
Where nature is profuse of smiles.
or the keen north whose furclad Host
Ranged upon the Arctic Coast:-
Beneath the light of moon and stars
Wait the day spring from afar
And in their long & tedious night
In Visions - catch the solar light
