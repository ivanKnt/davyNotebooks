I
I view the cloud tinged by
the western light
I view the cloud tinged by the western |sun| light
With hues etherial; where the purple light |light|
And golden radiance, undefinable
Blend us in one creation - As I view
My eye is filled my mind is satisfied.
This moving, changing & insensate [thing],
Is to my soul a comforter, A Source
Of joy & Hope.- Upon its fringed edge
Imagination frames |My fancy images| the falling dew,
Refreshing all that lives. -
A moment gone.
As with
philosophy passed by. - As with a
painters eye.
