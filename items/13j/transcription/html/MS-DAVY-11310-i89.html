Creative noble free & kind
The light the spirit of the |mind.|
And mayst thou Lovely woman |give|
Feelings which shall for ever live
The images by passion caught
The eloquence which kindles |thought|
Which strength to weakness can |impart|
And rouse again the exhausted |heart|
Which & like the refreshening streams |that flow|
From Cotopaxi crowned with snow
Wakening where Quitos plains |expand|
Life upon the burning sand
creating by its balmy power
The dewy herb & and odorous flower.

Cotopaxi

Cotopaxi is an active stratovolcano in the Andes Mountains, in Ecuador. The first European to climb it was Alexander Von Humboldt in 1802.

Quito

Quito, Ecuador's capital, sits high in the Andean foothills at an altitude of 2,850m. It is constructed on the foundations of an ancient Incan city.
