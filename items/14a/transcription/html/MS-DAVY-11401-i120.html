Minerva

The Roman goddess of wisdom, justice, law, victory, and the sponsor of arts, trade, and strategy. Minerva is not a patron of violence such as Mars, but of strategic war.

✕

Phebus

Or Apollo in Greek; mythological god of god of archery, music, medicine, the sun, and poetry.

Vulcan

The Roman and Greek god of fire and the forge, and mythical inventor of smithing and metal working.

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

Tin

Symbol/formula: Sn

A silvery-coloured metal, tin is soft enough to be cut with little force, and a bar of tin can be bent by hand with little effort.
