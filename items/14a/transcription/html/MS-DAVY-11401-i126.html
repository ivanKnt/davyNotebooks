Flint

Occasionally flintstone. A sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral quartz, categorised as the variety of chert that occurs in chalk or marly limestone.

✕

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

Herodotus

Greek historian and geographer who wrote a detailed account of the Greco-Persian Wars - one the earliest existing historical texts.
