Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

✕

Sienite

Syenite. A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock with a general composition similar to that of granite, but deficient in quartz, which, if present at all, occurs in relatively small concentrations.

✕

Porphyry

A hard, igneous purplish-red rock in which crystals of feldspar are embedded in a fine-grained groundmass.

✕

Feldspar

An abundant rock-forming mineral typically occurring as colourless or pale-coloured crystals and consisting of aluminosilicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium.

✕

Mica

A shiny silicate mineral with a layered structure, found as minute scales in granite and other rocks, or as crystals.

✕

Schorl

A form of tourmaline, a crystalline silicate mineral group in which boron is compounded with elements such as aluminium, iron, magnesium, sodium, lithium, or potassium.
