& there is a strong analogy for
believing in an infinity of
past existences which must
have had connexion; - &
human life may be regarded
as a type of infinite &
immortal life & its succession
of sleep & dreams as a type
of the changes of death & birth
to which from it's nature
it is liable. That the ideas
belonging to the mind were
originally gained from
those classes of sensations
called organs it is impossible
to deny, as it is impossible
to deny that mathematical
truth, depend upon the
signs which express them:
but these signs are not
themselves |the| truths, nor are
the organs the mind.
