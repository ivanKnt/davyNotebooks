July 10 Caught 3 or 4 grayling
& Trout last night. It is
this day cloudy & comparatively
[underline]cool[/underline] - T out of doors -
68° At 2 T has fallen to 65°
Moisture is at 58 -
a cool evening Went to
the Traun caught 4 Gr
& 1 Trout. The largest
Grayling 15 inches by 8 1/2.
weighed 1 lb 1/4. Another 13 inches
and 1/2 weighed 15 oz -
The Trout was like a
[misc_347]Par[/misc_347] with 12 blue marks
