the Sources of the Traun
Einem see. Lambath see
Laggen see Cammersee
Very large char caught in
Laggen see
9 |yesterday| came to Ischl 8 bathed
after dinner with I think
bad effects yesterday, changed my
lodging to apartment on the top
of the hill comfortable cool
and with a beautiful view.
10 shall not bathe again till the
tongue is cleaner and pulse restored
to standard and then shall use
warmer and shallow water for a much
shorter time.
