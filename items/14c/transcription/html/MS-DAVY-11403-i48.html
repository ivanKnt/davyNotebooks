the road saw some fish
in the beautiful bright
green water & soon caught
three graylings of about
a pound each -
But the greater number
would not rise at the
fly. Saw no flies either
on the stream or on the
Traun . - T at 3 60
M 57 -
14 Sat. pulse 73 b b
T. 62 M.57 at 9 -
After B P75. - Bowells
much deranged and & have been
since my bath. Began
Chocolate for breakfast
Went on the Halstadt See
