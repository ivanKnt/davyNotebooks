quere may not the shaking
of the carriage be a cause
of the increased feverish
symptoms and pain in the back
yet at Aussee I rode every
day on worse roads, but
I had a sprung carriage -
It is a bright day. I shall rest
all this day. -
I dined at 1/2 past 2 now
at half past 4 my pulse is 88
so that it has been quickened
by the loss of blood.
At 6 o'clock I reposed in shady
E window to the east wind
which feels fresh 72° F M55
difference 17. -
