Shoulder |& yellow tongue| and white faeces this
morning & Gum of iodine -
T. above 70 - 71 in E W
moist 63 ° - T. - m.
in a draught of air 60 -
Then the table in my room 68 T.
or 20 C.
Took my 3 continuous
bath this day - 3 unpaid
pulse after 78.
Fished below Ischl caught
7 small trout 1 Grayling -
Nothing but small fishes rose
It was a most beautiful
evening - tongue cleaner

- 20. Tongue far cleaner
It appears to me that the
skin of the left leg is beginning
to be sore -

Iodine

Symbol/formula: I

An element and the heaviest of the group of stable halogens. Bernard Courtois was the first to isolate the substance. Whilst in Paris Davy investigated the substance, and proved it to be a chemical element describing his results in a paper sent to the Royal Society. This caused disputes between Davy and Joseph Louis Gay-Lussac, who had also been researching the element.
