53
bright and beautiful day
Caught this evening far down
in the Traun . 7 fish one
good trout & 1 Grayling but
under a pound.
The fish did not rise till
after 7 o'clock. 21. Very hot day T. in
the outer window 78 - 79.
in the room 68, 69.
at 4 at 80 in the outer
window in shade.
Went out at 5 1/2 & fished
but was stopped by a great
thunderstorm.
22 T. 70-69 in room
6 bath unpaid, showry
day -
