61
satellites, become planets
planets, comets, comets
nebulae, nebulae suns.
& their inhabitants rise
from a dark & miserable
sensitive existence to
one of firm intellect
& the most exalted reason.
July 2 went to the small
stream which joins the Traun
at the wood catch called
Ischlen Rottenbach
raised & hooked several
Grayling caught two
& one Trout [was]
driven home by a thunder
storm. at 1/4 before 8
