July 3. T. from 68 to 73
[horizontal rule]
Reflection 'antecedant
to the vision - melancholy
view of the author.
In his view only circles
of identical existence -
Men like ants or bees
generations succeeding to
each other; but
the same result &
no improvement &
the houses of one race
founded upon the forms
of another.
Empires falling like towns
