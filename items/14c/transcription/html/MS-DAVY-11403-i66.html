July 4. Weather bright
& a bright sun . -
In the vision
shewn the truly famous
the discoverer of Corn, of
cultivation, of domesticating
animals- of malleable
iron of gunpowder
of paper of statuary
of painting of gunpowder
To George 10 Gulden
i.e. more than £1.
5 Caught last night this evening
4th nine Trout & Grayling
principally in the Rottenbach
They rose well.
