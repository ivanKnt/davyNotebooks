Ironstone

A sedimentary rock, either deposited directly as a ferruginous sediment or created by chemical replacement, that contains a substantial proportion of an iron ore compound.

✕

Cast iron

A class of iron–carbon alloys with a carbon content more than 2% and silicon content around 1-3%.

✕

Steel

Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon with improved strength and fracture resistance compared to other forms of iron.
