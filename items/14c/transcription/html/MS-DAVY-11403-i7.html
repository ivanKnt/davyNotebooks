5
April - 19. At Landshut
saw the Perca - Zingel living
& had some for dinner.
colour browner than in Block
a very good fish
the largest about 3/4 of a pound
to examine & draw tomorrow. -
Yesterday at Ratisbonne

- Saw a huco amongst
Carp which looked pale
red; but this colour
explains the name Rodfisc
the great time for these
fish is in May. The
Danube probably rises
& they run up after the
first warm floods -
