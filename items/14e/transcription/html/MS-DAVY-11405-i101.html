Is destroyed by heat |that| which is not changeable
The Granite rock, the mightiest of the mighty
The burden of the earth, yields to the dews
Of heaven:- the particles of which intangible
Act through the lapse of ages with a |strength|
Sure and irresistible -
So worlds are worn away! So Monuments
Raised by the labour of a myriad race
Vanish before the frosts, the sunshine
And the winds of heaven!
