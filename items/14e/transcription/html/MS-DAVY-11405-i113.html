Corrected Stanza
to be added to the Poem
on the death of Ld. byron .
Now passing near those high & blessed
abodes
Where Beings of a higher |nobler| nature
move
In fields of |purest| light. Where intellectual |brightest|
rays
of Glory shine. In power allied to Gods
Whose minds etherial & undying prove
that
Truth in its brightness, & Eternal blaze
^ Which man sees dimly [xxxxxxx]
Whose hearts are warm with everlasting love.
Whose minds in Life & in fruition prove
That unconsuming & etherial blaze
Flowing from returning to Eternal love

George GordonByron

From 1798 6th Lord Byron. Famous poet who knew both Humphry and Jane Davy in London and Italy; he wrote a slightly risqué skit on their marriage in 1812. FRSL.
