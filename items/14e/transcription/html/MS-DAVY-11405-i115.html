To Love.
Thou who art so cunning
So bright & heavenly fair
Which is thy beginning
And ending. It is care.
First a sigh then a smile
The honest vows then guile
Amidst the moonlight bowers
And wreathes of rosy flowers.
Then come jealous hours
Then rapture then fears
And thou endest in showers
of [sparks] or of tears
-
Or
And then endst in a shower of tears
Of despair or repentance or joy.
