They do not kindle at my glory
They do not love my name in story
The selfish feeling uncontrasted
In narrow minds usurps the throne
Which in the spirit lofty bold:
Is little known or known alone
To be disposed & conquered
– Mounts bay
Yet that to which the mind alone
Applied its own [creative] measure
Though facts & feelings all are gone
be still as age a sacred treasure
In solitude T'was mine to own
The mighty dead & the unknown
And pure & hallowed sympathies
For them took their were work
to rise
