Manhood power & passion. Sunshine
& the full leaf & blossom. -
The productive autumn of life
its quiet peaceful & sunny days
The fruit formed now permanent
The brightening of intellect & its
highest perfection before failure
In winter coldness & feebleness
Roots or hoarded fruit the
stores of mind. In other respects
a second infancy.

How much is life in all its varied hues
And course & current like a mighty stream
And there are streams which are for ever pure
And which perennial from their sources gush
With the same force & brightness
fed from heaven
Without a [taint] of earth so
