George GordonByron

From 1798 6th Lord Byron. Famous poet who knew both Humphry and Jane Davy in London and Italy; he wrote a slightly risqué skit on their marriage in 1812. FRSL.

✕

Cain

A biblical figure in the Book of Genesis. He is the firstborn son of Adam and Eve and brother of Abel.

✕

Fintl

Possibly Davy is phonetically spelling 'Vintl'. He was at Steinach, apparently intending to cross the Brenner pass, on the 26th July 1827. By the 31st he was in Sillian, in the Puster Valley, and about halfway between Steinach and Sillian are Niedervintl (Lower Vintl) and Obervintl (Upper Vintl).
