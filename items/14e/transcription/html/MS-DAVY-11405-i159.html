Ravenna - April 5
When life was newer then every
verdant tree
Seemed cloaked |trimmed| with laurels which
to my free head
Seemed to offer themselves,
And every |humble| mead seemed was
cloaked with flowers.
I saw the rose; but never saw
the thorn.
I saw the brilliant [xx] adder |snake|
but not its poison fang. |never felt|
Its poison fang, or suffered or even its
rude harsh scales.

& soft its sound
A voice of praise, |or love| & every flower

of festal joy.
