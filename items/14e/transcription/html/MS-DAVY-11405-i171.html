April 1 Ravenna
Then on his mind |soul| flashed
the bright images
Of what He was in youth
when power & beauty formed |moved|
[ his livid looks]
his turning mind active mind
Which moved a body
To seek for power and beauty
& the ideal was far more
vivid than the real now.
When power was his the
undying power of mind
And beauty such as
in the god of light imaged
in marble by the grecian
hand
called for admiring

That e'en as a [wish] Would be nothing
at all. –
