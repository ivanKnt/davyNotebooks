183
Thought.
The hope that other ages
which in light
More glorious than those
feeble beams which shine
in this wan twilight
Shall distinctly see what
we imagine
And but feebly hope
The world intractable in which
alone
Wisdom is found. the life
& light of things
The breath divine creating
power divine
The One of which the human
intellect
Is but a type as feeble
as that image
