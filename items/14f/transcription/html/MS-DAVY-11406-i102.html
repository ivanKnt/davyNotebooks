The Holy Sepulchre

The Church of the Holy Sepulchre: fourth-century church in the Christian Quarter of the Old City of Jerusalem. It is considered to be the holiest site for Christians in the world, as it is said to contain the site of Jesus's crucifixion and the tomb in which he was buried prior to his resurrection.
