travertine |around it| though formed
by a variable source
from the most perishable
materials have hardened
by time & form the
most perfect remains
of the greatest ruins
in the eternal city.
such as the triumphal
arches & the collosaeum
owe their duration to
this source.
Then from all we know
this lake |except in some change in dimension| continues nearly
in the same state
in which it was described 1700
|years ago| by Pliny & I have no
doubt contains the same
