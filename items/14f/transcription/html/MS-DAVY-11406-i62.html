Lias Limestone

The Lias Group or Lias is sequence of rock strata found in a large area of western Europe. It consists of marine limestones, shales, marls and clays.

Muschelkalk

The Muschelkalk is a sequence of sedimentary rock strata in the geology of central and western Europe.
