The Stranger. One of you
referred in our conversation
this morning to a vision
which had some relation
to the subject of our discussion
& I was promised some
information on this subject.
Philalethes . I willingly
give it. I immediately
gave a sketch of my
vision & of the opinions
which had been expressed
by Ambrosio on the
subject of the early
History of Man & the
termination of our discussion
