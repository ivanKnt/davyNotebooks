Upon the pastoral banks of |Forth or Tweed |
For never yet midst mortal men |was known|
A spirit more powerful, Not even his
The Greek of Syracuse , whose mighty mind
Wielded at will the gross machinery
Of this material world & gave it forms
Of toys to please a King or instruments
To conquer or destroy - To which in [mid] |career|
The giant strength of the all conquering |People|
