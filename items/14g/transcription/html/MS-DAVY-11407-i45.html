Some minds there are with tints so |heavenly bright|
No cloud can change them & no |shade obscure.|
They deep in misfortune & in sunshine |joy| |acquire|
A more ethereal hue. So e'en was
thy waves
O lovely Leman which beneath |the storm|
When all above is black |& terrible|
Gains but a deeper sapphire |& in sunshine|
Seems bright as is the illumined |sky above them|
