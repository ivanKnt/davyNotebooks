The end of May the
Snow clad mountains above
from which the Velino
rises are bold & rugged
but the hills want
wood & the green freshness
of Styria or Switzerland
& the rock they present
is not [sufficiently] bold precipitous
or mighty
to add to the sublimity
of the scene - it gives
the idea of barreness &
not of boldness - They
talked of trouts in the
Velino I saw none rise
Pesciara , the stones on the side of the
great road many of them rival the Rosso antico

Aterno-Pescara

The Aterno-Pescara is a river system in Abruzzo, eastern central Italy. The river is known as the Aterno near its source in the mountains, but takes the name Pescara, actually a tributary, nearer the city of Pescara and the Adriatic Sea.

Rosso antico
