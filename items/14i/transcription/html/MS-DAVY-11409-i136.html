Nothing can exceed the beauty of the waterfall of Terni

Faraday writes about staying at Terni waterfall all day on 3rd June 1814; see The life and letters of Faraday, ed. Bence Jones, Vol. 1, page 138. Byron also visited the Cascata delle Marmore, an artificial waterfall created by the Romans, and wrote about it in Childe Harold’s Pilgrimage, IV. 69.
