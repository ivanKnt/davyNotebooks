Schill

A German name for the pike-perch/zander (Sander lucioperca), found in Lake Ammersee.

Dorset

A county in southwest England. It’s known for the Jurassic Coast, a long stretch on the English Channel where the cliffs contain many fossils, and rock formations show millions of years of geological history.

Spigola

Used by Davy as a name for the European seabass or possibly the painted comber (Italian).
