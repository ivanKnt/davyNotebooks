A billow in a stormy sea & clear sky breaking
in the surf, gloomy within, but on its surface
glittering in all the glory of the rainbow, a Sort
of creation between the heavens &the earth
produced by the wrath & tumult of the elements
but again in a state of repose & of beauty.
all its violence, gloom, foam & frenzy belonging to
its earthy origin, but its glory, its beauty, its immor-
tality belonging to its Divine origin-
