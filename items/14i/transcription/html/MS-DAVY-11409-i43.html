Phil. The information we have already
received from you proves to me that Chemistry
has been your favourite pursuit. I am surprised
at this. The higher mathematics & pure physics
appear to me to offer much more noble objects
of contemplation & fields of discovery & practically
considered the results of the Chemist are
much more humble, belonging principally to
The Apothecaries shop or the Kitchin.
