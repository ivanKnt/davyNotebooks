JohnDalton

Mancunian chemist and natural philosopher who lectured at the Royal Institution. He formulated a version of the atomic theory of matter much disliked by Davy. FRSL.

✕

Jeremias BenjaminRichter

German chemist who worked on the idea of definite proportions.

✕

Ammonium nitrate

Symbol/formula: NH4NO3

A white crystalline salt consisting of ions of ammonium and nitrate; previously called phosnitrate of ammoniac by Davy.

✕

Nitrous oxide

Symbol/formula: N2O

Later known as 'laughing gas'. Davy discovered that nitrous oxide was respirable when not contaminated with nitric oxide (NO). Davy researched into the physiological effects of nitrous oxide.

✕

the two Higgins

the Irish chemists: William Higgins (1763–1825) and his uncle, Bryan Higgins (1741–1818).
