HarrietMellon

The wife of Thomas Coutts, the banker, who died in 1822. She was widely celebrated for her beauty and met Coutts when she was acting at the Duke Street Theatre. She claimed to have come from a poor family in Cork but was left Coutts's entire fortune, making her the richest woman of her age.
