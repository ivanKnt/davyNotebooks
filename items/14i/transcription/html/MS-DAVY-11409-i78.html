Covent Garden

Covent Garden is a district in London, on the eastern fringes of the West End, between St Martin's Lane and Drury Lane. It is associated with the former fruit-and-vegetable market in the central square (now a popular shopping and tourist site), and with the Royal Opera House, itself known as "Covent Garden". By the 18th century it had become notorious for its abundance of brothels
