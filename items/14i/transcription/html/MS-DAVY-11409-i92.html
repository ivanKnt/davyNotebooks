George IV

Eldest son of George III and Prince of Wales. Served as Prince Regent during the King's madness, 1811-1820, thereafter King of England, Scotland and Ireland. A major patron of the arts, he personally commanded Davy to go to Naples to oversee the unrolling of the Herculaneum papyri. PatronRSL.
