AlessandroVolta

Professor of experimental physics at the University of Pavia. At the end of the eighteenth century he Invented the Voltaic pile which produced electric current and which Davy shortly dubbed the battery. Davy described meeting him in 1814 in notebook 14/I, p.38. ForMemRSL.

GuiseppePiazzi

Professor of astronomy at the University of Palermo. Discovered the first asteroid, which he named Ceres, in 1801. ForMemRSL

Voltaic

Vesta

Vesta is one of the largest objects in the asteroid belt, with a mean diameter of 525 kilometres. It was discovered by the German astronomer Heinrich Wilhelm Matthias Olbers on 29 March 1807 and is named after Vesta, the virgin goddess of home and hearth from Roman mythology.
