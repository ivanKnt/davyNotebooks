sterlet

The sterlet (Acipenser ruthenus) is a relatively small species of sturgeon from Eurasia native to large rivers that flow into the Black Sea, Azov Sea, and Caspian Sea, as well as rivers in Siberia as far east as Yenisei.

Senior army commander and politician under Queen Anne. Created 1st Duke of Marlborough in 1702, he commanded the army at the Battle of Blenheim (1704). For that victory Parliament voted funds to build Blenheim Palace.
