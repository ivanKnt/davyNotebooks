June 22 - Vock la bruck - "A
great thunderstorm in the
night & the rain continues.
Ther: 68o - Went to the source
of the Ager , that is, where
it is poured out by the
Cammer see , a fine
sapphire (pale blue) stream
full of Grayling . Whilst
a great thunder storm was
brewing & falling, I
caught a vast many
Grayling - beautiful fish
with yellow bellies & sides
some a pound & half -
Caught so many that
I cannot count them. This
part of the See not so
beautiful as the same
part of the Traun see ;
The river I think clearer
& about half the size. - .
