Carniola

The Alpine region whose capital was Laybach (now Ljubljana). In 1827, Carniola was a Duchy within the Austrian Kingdom of Illyria.

Sava River

The Sava River, 990km in length, rises in Slovenia (Carnolia) and is the largest tributary of the Danube by volume of water.
