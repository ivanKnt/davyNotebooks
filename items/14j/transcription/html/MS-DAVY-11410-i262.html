danger & much dripping &
one death, that of an un-

in the morning, my
experiments ruined &
misfortune to aggravate
the misery of sickness. -
We had gone to bed in
sickness, storms & darkness
on rising, all was calm
beauty & repose - We
were in the bosom of a
bason rather than a bay, -

beautifully green and myriads
