Dec. 8. took milk from the Coffee
house. - went to Pantana
Borghese Found some snipes
shot very ill - killed only 2 or 3.
The needle placing itself at
right lines to the [symbol] I have
no doubt is an electrial
& not a magnetic phenomenon
Decr 9 - A sirocco . -
Robert is gone & paid - Hired
a Servant for tomorrow at
8 Naps a month, paid a half
a month for my stable.
Decr 10. Lewis came -
went to Pantana Borghese
found some snipes . shot ill -
killed a Woodcock & seven snipes
Did not return till 6 - Rain
& was wet. - corn & hay.
Decr 11. Coffee finished. this

Pantano Borghese

On the outskirts of Rome south of Tivoli, an estate that since 1613 has belonged to the Borghese family.

Napoleons

Napoléon is a colloquial term for former French gold coins which came in a range of denominations. The name most commonly refers to the 20 Franc denomination issued by Napoleon I.

Woodcock

A large wading bird; its genus name Scolopax is Latin for a snipe.
