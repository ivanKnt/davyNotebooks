Adige

The second-longest river in Italy, which rises in the province of South Tyrol, near the Italian border with Austria and Switzerland.

Brenta River

The Brenta River has its source in Trentino, north-east Italy, and flows into the Adriatic. It rises from the lakes Levico and Caldonazzo.

Lago di Levico

Lago di Levico is a lake in Trentino, north-east Italy. It is known as the 'Little Brother' of the larger nearby lake, Lago di Caldonazzo. Both lakes are the source of the Brenta River.

Lago di Caldonazzo

Lago di Cadonazzo is a lake in Trentino, north-east Italy. It lies alongside Lago di Levico. Both lakes form the source of the Brenta River.
