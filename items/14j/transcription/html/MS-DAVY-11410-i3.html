Rome . Novre 19. - a beautiful
day. intend 1. ride to S Peters.
arrived here yesterday at Serny's . -
Wrote to P.
- Drew on Torlonia by Drummond
for £200 - which produce
925 Dollars - & took 25 Dollars
remain 900.
Nov r 20 A beautiful day.
Thanks to Exp. my Horse [x] fell with
me yesterday - & I hurt my ancle
(right) severely. applied 2 leeches
last night to the ancle & 4 to
the anus & lost a good deal of blood.
Novr 21. A beautiful day bright & blue
feel rather better -
The 2d Dialogue may
refer to the scenery at
Naples & the Volcanic
a view -
