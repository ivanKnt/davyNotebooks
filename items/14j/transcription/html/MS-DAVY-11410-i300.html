on the 20 th. of that month
he had the severe attack
which ultimately proved
fatal. That morning [ as
he told me afterwards)
he felt better than usual
his pulse about 68; the
Tongue clean, - the ordinary
functions of the body
well performed; After
break fast he had
sat some time dictating
an addition to the
6 th Dialogue ; when
he had finished, he
attempted to rise to
go into his bed room
