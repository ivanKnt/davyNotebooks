In tumuli, & most impressive
of all the vast aqueducts
running parallel towards
the city,- their [lines]
[broken] & their lofty
arches giving the idea
of enormous temples-
As was our custom we
got out at the end
of our drive & walked
a little way on the
Campagna for exercise,
& whilst we walked
conversed on the subject
of the extraordinary
scenery around us,-
so ancient, & yet, the
soil of volcanic sand,
& still glittering
