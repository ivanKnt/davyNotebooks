JohnDavy

Humphry Davy's brother, born in Penzance. Spent some time working with Davy in the Royal Institution before studying medicine at Edinburgh University. Appointed an Army physician he was elevated to the rank of Inspector General of Army Hospitals in the West Indies. Following his brother's death, Davy's notebooks came into his possession which he used in writing biographical studies that strongly defended his reputation. FRSL.
