61
snipes only .-
25 Christmass day cloudy & a sirocco
shall stay at home feel oppressed & lame
To dwell upon the
Spectacle of the return
of Pio VII - as shewing
the effects of religion
the ecstasy of the Roman
People on receiving his
benediction May 24 1814.
Old Men & Matrons
sobbing like children
Kneeling in the Mud
& the rain & Umbrellas
forgotten - He blesses
the Rosary of the Unknown
Who will never part
with it

snipe

Medium sized, skulking wading birds with short legs and long, straight bills.

A warm Mediterranean wind that comes from the Sahara.

Barnaba Niccolò Maria LuigiChiaramonti

Pope Pius VII, 1800-1823, held prisioner by Buonparte, 1809-1813. In February 1814 Davy and his party stayed in the same inn that Pius had used the week before on his way back to Rome.

The Unknown

A fictional character in Humphry Davy's Consolations in Travel (1830).
