81
Minnows & [gen] of fish [Chinese]
plan change of water
7 & 8 Rain incessant
worked at the Dialogues
& Salmonia . |Probably sent|
to Murray
9. A fine day - went to
[Forno nuovo] . the old
Woodcock haunt. found
one only - shot a snipe .
& missed one.
Thought this morning; respiration
& nutrition make the system
fit for nervous action - all
the elements probably provide
for the brain its materials
of [affecting] the mind -
then spiritual essences
so infinitely numerous
assume & change at pleasure
of the Deity their frames

JohnMurray

Publisher of many of the key literary and scientific texts of the time, including by Byron, Austen, Davy. Published The Quarterly Journal of Science (closely likned to the Royal Institution) as well as Davy's Six Discourses (1827), Salmonia (1828) and Consolations in Travel (1830).

✕

Woodcock

A large wading bird; its genus name Scolopax is Latin for a snipe.

✕

snipe

Medium sized, skulking wading birds with short legs and long, straight bills.
