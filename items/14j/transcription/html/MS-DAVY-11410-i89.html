JaneDavy

Daughter of Charles Kerr, an Antiguan merchant who made his fortune in highly dubious circumstances. Following his death she moved to Britain where she married Schukbrugh Apreece who died in 1807. After running a successful literary salon in Edinburgh she moved to London where she met Davy and they married on 11 April 1812 two days after he had been knighted by the Prince Regent.

Steel

Steel is an alloy of iron and carbon with improved strength and fracture resistance compared to other forms of iron.

Tow

Short or broken fibres (as of flax, hemp, or synthetic material) that are used especially for yarn, twine, or stuffing. A wetted version of this substance was often used as a conductor in early electrical experiments, such as Davy's on the Voltaic pile.
