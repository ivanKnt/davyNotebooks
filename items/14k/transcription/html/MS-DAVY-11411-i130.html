valde miserabilis

very pitiful (Latin)

✕

Voluntas est Dei op. om

"It is the will of God, maker of all things" or "all-powerful and all-knowing." (Latin). The abbreviation could be "opificis omnium", or "omnipotentis omniscientis."

✕

Thomas Stamford BingleyRaffles

British colonial official who served as the governor of the Dutch East Indies between 1811 and 1816 and lieutenant-governor of Bencoolen between 1818 and 1824. Because he had opposed (or restricted) the slave trade in his colonial administration, he was refused burial inside a parish church, and his gravesite was lost until 1914.
