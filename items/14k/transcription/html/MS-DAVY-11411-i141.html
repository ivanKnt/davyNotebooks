ad curand infl. ocul

Probably: "ad curandam inflammationem oculorum" - in order to cure an inflammation of the eyes (Latin)

blue dun (fly), bleak and Miller's Thumb

Small fish used as bait

Huco

Hucho, a genus of large piscivorous salmonid fish known as taimens, and is closely related to Pacific trout and lenoks. Native to the cold rivers and other freshwater habitats in Eurasia

✕

Grayling

The grayling Thymallus thymallus belongs in a cold-water fish family of 6 species distributed across parts of the Northern hemisphere

Traun

Traunfall in Desselbrunn, Upper Austria. Memoirs contains a poem Davy wrote about them, also in notebook 14E.
