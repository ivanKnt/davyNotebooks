Joseph HeinrichStratil

A naturalist who was the first to recognise the significance of certain burial mounds in Carniola, about which he published a report in 1827. In 1825 Stratil interviewed several persons from Sittich who reported observing a caught proteus giving birth to several young ones and wrote a formal protocol which was published by Dr. Michahelles in 1831. Stratil had previously caught and kept several protei himself. This may explain why Davy, who was interested in the protei, had Stratil's address noted down and added the olm's Latin name "proteus anguinus".
