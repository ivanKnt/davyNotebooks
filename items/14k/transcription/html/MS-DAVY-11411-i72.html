Comparing the temperatures shown by a thermometer with a dry bulb and one with a wetted bulb was and still is a method of measuring the humidity content of the air. The evaporation cools the wet thermometer, and since the evaporation rate depends on the vapour content of the air, the temperature difference allows to determine the humidity. If 60.5 and 53.5 are the dry and wet temperature, respectively (the difference therefore 7 degrees), the relative humidity is now about 64 %

Slate

Slate is a fine-grained, foliated, homogeneous, metamorphic rock derived from an original shale-type sedimentary rock composed of clay or volcanic ash through low-grade, regional metamorphism
