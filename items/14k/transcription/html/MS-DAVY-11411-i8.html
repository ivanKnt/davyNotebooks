Migration of Animals
There is some intimate
connexion between the
state of the Atmosphere in
different regions of the Globe
the double snipe the quail
& the Woodcock never leave
their haunts in the
South till the Temperature
of the North is fit for
them & this year the migratory
birds are all late the snow
which fell in the end of
Feby still covering the
north of Europe in the middle
of March.

Double Snipe

An old name for a type of snipe.

Quails

A type of small gamebird belonging to the same order as turkeys and chickens; often killed for sport and food.

✕

Woodcock

A large wading bird; its genus name Scolopax is Latin for a snipe.
