June 13
The water of the Danube
at six oClock near
Stein 61°; Air 59
no mist nor vapour
At 8 oClock the easterly wind
began to blow & the thermometer
in the shade though the sun
was bright sunk to
56, The water 61. 1/2
some little appearance
of mist
