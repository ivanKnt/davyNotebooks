goitres

A lump or swelling at the front of the neck and enlargement of the thyroid in the neck which used to affect large parts of the population in the Alpine region, due to a lack of iodine in the Alpine soil and consequently in crops and fruit. Davy sometimes also uses this term to describe geological features.
