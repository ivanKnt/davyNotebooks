In the immense lakes of Hungary
Trees on the banks
Trees at Kaposvar in the middle
of a marsh which can only
be approached in very severe
winter. a forest where a
Boy was found; who lived upon
fish & had no language
& had probably been lost when
very young -
- By an inundation of the
Neusidler See, a large forest
of oaks was destroyed - I saw
this.
Prince Esterhazys [coal] near
