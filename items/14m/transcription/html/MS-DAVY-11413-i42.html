the Mur - & now -
18. Stopped at Frisach
& went quail shooting
a bright hot day.
with some wind. shot
very ill killed 3 quails
only & 1 L rail missed
I suppose 8 or 10 quails -
& a land rail
19. Promise of a cloudy
day intend to go to
Clagenfurth
The inn at st Veit
appears good.
