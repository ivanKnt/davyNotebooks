Augt 21. Bright sunshine
but a cool strong breeze
T. 67. 68.- Went out
with the Dogs found only
1 quail & 1 Rail. got
only 2 Shots at the quail
which I could not find.
In the evening went again
for an hour above found
many quails - & 1 rail
shot 2 quails -
Augt 22. Went to the
upper marsh. found no
snipes &c; but great
number of quails shot
5 Quails. or rather found
5 shot 2 or three more.
It rained. T. 64
Augt. 23. T in window 45 at
nine. It is snowing on the
mountains. at 2 - 50 T.-

✕

Quails

A type of small gamebird belonging to the same order as turkeys and chickens; often killed for sport and food.

✕

snipe

Medium sized, skulking wading birds with short legs and long, straight bills.
