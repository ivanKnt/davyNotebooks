Sepr 5 Rain all last night
& still rains at 9
Sepr 6. The same kind of weather
rain rain rain
Went out on the new road
in carriage got 6 shots, shot
2 Quails & a land rail -
Rails of passage came.
Sepr 7 Went out on the new
& Neuestadtle road
Killed a quail & a
joketta that had the
usual larva in her stomach
[pencil sketch of larva] this
Found one or two more
Jokette at which I did
not fire. This joketta
weighed 7 oz.
A fine day
Saldato fin la matina
di 7. Sept. pidi

Novo Mesto

Formerly Neustadtl, Novo Mesto is the seventh-largest city of Slovenia. It is the economic and cultural centre of the traditional region of Lower Carniola and the seat of the City Municipality of Novo Mesto. It lies on a bend of the Krka River, close to the border with Croatia

Joketten

Davy phonetically transcribes 'ciochetta' in Lombard dialect. It is Gallinago media, or, the Great Snipe (Italian)

JosephineDetela

The daughter of the landlord of the Laybach (Ljubljana) inn at which Davy stayed in 1827 and 1828. Davy nicknamed her 'Pepina'. In Consolations in Travel (1830) he portrayed her in idealised terms as his nurturing nurse.
