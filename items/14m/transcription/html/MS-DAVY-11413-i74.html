Joketten

Davy phonetically transcribes 'ciochetta' in Lombard dialect. It is Gallinago media, or, the Great Snipe (Italian)

✕

Quails

A type of small gamebird belonging to the same order as turkeys and chickens; often killed for sport and food.

✕

snipe

Medium sized, skulking wading birds with short legs and long, straight bills.

Novo Mesto

Formerly Neustadtl, Novo Mesto is the seventh-largest city of Slovenia. It is the economic and cultural centre of the traditional region of Lower Carniola and the seat of the City Municipality of Novo Mesto. It lies on a bend of the Krka River, close to the border with Croatia
