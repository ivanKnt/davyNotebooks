Instinct
Poetes you transiently
referred |Halieus| yesterday to that
Instinct of Salmons which
induces them to run
up river from the sea
on the approach of rain
You have had so many
opportunities of attending
to the instincts of
the inferior animals
in general that I
should be very glad
to hear your opinion
on that very curious
subject the nature
& development of instincts
