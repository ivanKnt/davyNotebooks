It appears to me that
the best plan with the
Book or Archaeus or Summer
days |of youth| Will be to make
it Colloquial & [Colludial].
I have two or three
+ Dialogues connected with
the Vision in the Colloseum
one on the Sidereal system
& on the results of motion
in this system.
Another Vision may
be geological & two
Dialogues may be founded
upon this -
It may be a question
whether the Proteus
& Hermes or Archaeus
or an Animal Chem.

✕

Reference

Humphry Davy (1830)Second Dialogue. Consolations in Travel, or the Last Days of Philosopher.

✕

the Proteus

Known as the olm, a cave salamandera, which Davy sent specimens of to the natural historian Sir Everard Home.

✕

Hermes

The messenger god in ancient Greek religion and mythology.
