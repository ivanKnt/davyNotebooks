5 - The County of Ww & that
of Longford abound in small Lakes
which are surrounded by bogs
& on the shores of them
at low water in dry seasons the Horns
of Deer are discovered in great
abundance the Horns & bones
in great varit quantities
This Country now so bare
was anciently an immense
forest & it is an object
which might employ speculation
as worthily as many other
objects whether it the great
change was owing to the
slow operations of Natures
decay - or to some
great convulsion or inundation
