Edgeworthstown

Edgeworthstown is a town in County Longford, Ireland. It is named after the Edgeworth family, who lived on an estate there. It was the home of Richard Lovell Edgeworth, Associate at the Medical Pneumatic Institution, and his daughter Anna Beddoes, née Edgeworth.
