River Eske

The River Eske is a river in County Donegal, Ireland. It begins at Lough Eske in the southeast of the county before flowing mainly westwards to the town of Donegal and into the Atlantic Ocean via Donegal Bay. Donegal Town is the only major settlement through which it flows. Although Davy omits the final "e" it is clear from context he means this river, rather than the English or Scottish rivers spelt "Esk".
