Zeolitic basalt

Zeolites (minerals that contain mainly aluminum and silicon compounds) within basalt.

✕

Silicious

Symbol/formula: SiO2

Siliceous rocks: any of a group of sedimentary rocks that consist largely or almost entirely of silicon dioxide.

✕

Shale

Shale is a fine-grained, clastic sedimentary rock formed from mud that is a mix of flakes of clay minerals and tiny fragments of other minerals, especially quartz and calcite.

✕

Pyrites

A generic term for sulphide minerals.

✕

Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.

✕

Lapis Lydius

touch-stone; basanite; a variety of siliceous slate.
