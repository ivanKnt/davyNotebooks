Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.

St Thibery

Saint-Thibéry is a commune in the Hérault department in the Occitanie region of southern France. The village of Saint-Thibéry is built at the confluence of the Rivers Thongue and Hérault.
