Fairhead

Fairhead is a spectacular 5km long, 82m high cliff above the sea at Ballycastle, County Antrim, Northern Ireland. It is a dolerite sill.

Hornblende

A dark brown, black, or green mineral consisting of a silicate of calcium, magnesium, and iron, occurring in many igneous and metamorphic rocks.
