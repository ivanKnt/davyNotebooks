Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.

✕

Shale

Shale is a fine-grained, clastic sedimentary rock formed from mud that is a mix of flakes of clay minerals and tiny fragments of other minerals, especially quartz and calcite.
