Lead

Symbol/formula: Pb

A heavy metal that is denser than most common materials. Lead is soft and malleable, and also has a relatively low melting point.

✕

Iron

Symbol/formula: Fe

A strong, hard, magnetic silvery-grey metal. It is, by mass, the most common element on Earth, just ahead of oxygen, forming much of Earth's outer and inner core.

✕

Gossan

Gossan (eiserner hut or eisenhut) is intensely oxidized, weathered or decomposed rock, usually the upper and exposed part of an ore deposit or mineral vein. As noted by Davy, its name comes from Cornish mining slang.

✕

Gangue

In mining, gangue is the commercially worthless material that surrounds, or is closely mixed with, a wanted mineral in an ore deposit

Derbyshire

Derbyshire is a county in the East Midlands and includes part of the Peak District.
