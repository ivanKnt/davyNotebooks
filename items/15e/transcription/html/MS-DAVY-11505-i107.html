Slate

Slate is a fine-grained, foliated, homogeneous, metamorphic rock derived from an original shale-type sedimentary rock composed of clay or volcanic ash through low-grade, regional metamorphism

Limestone

Iron

Symbol/formula: Fe

A strong, hard, magnetic silvery-grey metal. It is, by mass, the most common element on Earth, just ahead of oxygen, forming much of Earth's outer and inner core.
