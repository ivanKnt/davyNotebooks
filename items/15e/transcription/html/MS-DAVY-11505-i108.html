Limestone

A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3).

✕

Sandstone

A sedimentary rock consisting of sand or quartz grains cemented together, typically red, yellow, or brown in colour.

✕

Shale

Shale is a fine-grained, clastic sedimentary rock formed from mud that is a mix of flakes of clay minerals and tiny fragments of other minerals, especially quartz and calcite.

✕

Lead

Symbol/formula: Pb

A heavy metal that is denser than most common materials. Lead is soft and malleable, and also has a relatively low melting point.
