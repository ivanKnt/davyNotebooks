Strontium

Symbol/formula: Sr

An alkaline earth metal, a soft silver-white yellowish metallic element that is highly chemically reactive. The metal forms a dark oxide layer when it is exposed to air.

Strontian

The main village in Sunart, an area in western Lochaber, in the Highlands of Scotland. It was where the mineral strontianite was discovered, and from which the element strontium was first isolated.

Loch Sunart

Loch Sunart is a sea loch on the west coast of Scotland. Loch Sunart is bounded to the north by the Sunart district of Ardnamurchan and to the south by the Morvern district. At 31 kilometres long, it is the longest sea loch in the Highland local government area.
