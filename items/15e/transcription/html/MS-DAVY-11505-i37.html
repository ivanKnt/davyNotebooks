CharlesHatchett

He made his money from coach building which he used to pursue his interests in chemistry and mineralogy in the course of which he isolated the chemical element now called niobium. He was a Manager of the Royal Institution, playing a key role especially in running its lecture programme during the 1800s. FRSL.

✕

WilliamBabington

Physician to Guy's Hospital and mineralogist, one of the founders of the Geological Society. A close friend of Davy, Babington was one of those who treated him during his severe illness of late 1807, early 1808. Shared his enthusiasm for angling. FRSL.

✕

Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

✕

Zeolite

Any member of a family of hydrated aluminosilicate minerals that contain alkali and alkaline-earth metals.
