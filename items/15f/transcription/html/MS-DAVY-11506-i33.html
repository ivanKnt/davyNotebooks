If aught can claim
From depth of feeling & from depth
of thought
Rises the purest strain of Poesy
And if a subject, high preeminent
Claims her most rapturous strain.
It is the Man
Who from the purest sympathy
& love
In will, in power devotes himself
to serve.
To benefit the public.
His is a purer hope than ever filled
The Conquerors burning heart high.
beating in the field
of Victory.
