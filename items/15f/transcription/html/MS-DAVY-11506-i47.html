To the paper that enfolded
Anna Hair
Pleasure is enhanced
by pain, but not pain
by pleasure – great &
glorious law of our nature
use of pain in giving
activity to the limbs of
the child the tendency of
pleasure is to create sloth.–
[Pencil sketch]
