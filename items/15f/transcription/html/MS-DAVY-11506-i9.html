Board of Agriculture

The Board of Agriculture (1793-1822) was a British voluntary association founded to promote agricultural improvement. Davy gave an annual course of lectures to the Board 1803-1812. Davy's career there was pushed by Joseph Banks.

✕

Reference

George Fordyce (1771)Elements of Agriculture and Vegetation. .

✕

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

✕

JohnEgerton

MP for Chester and from 1814 8th baronet. Landowner who found a vein of copper ore on his land and who asked Davy for advice.
