Killiney

Killiney is a coastal area of County Dublin, Ireland. In the 19th Century it was a popular seaside resort and many of the pebbles and rocks along Killiney Beach are examples of carboniferous limestone, some of which contain fossils of corals, sea shells and other sea life that existed millions of years ago.
