Sandstone

A sedimentary rock consisting of sand or quartz grains cemented together, typically red, yellow, or brown in colour.

Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.

✕

JamesMcDonnell

Radical Irish physician and supporter of literature who founded the Belfast Literary Society in 1801. Davy met him on one of his visits to Ireland.

✕

Mulattoes

Davy describes this as Dr. Ml.'s term for a mixed stone; this was an offensive term used for a person of mixed white and black ancestry and is used here as a geological term.
