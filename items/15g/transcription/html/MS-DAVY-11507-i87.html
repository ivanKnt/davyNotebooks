Greywacke

Copper

Symbol/formula: Cu

A soft, malleable, and ductile metal with very high thermal and electrical conductivity. A freshly exposed surface of pure copper has a pinkish-orange colour.

Limestone

A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3).

Charnwood Forest

The Charnwood Forest is a upland area in Leicestershire, the highest point being 278m. The geology is remarkable as Precambrian igneous rocks, some of the oldest rocks on earth, are seen as isolated outcrops, as in Davy's sketch.
