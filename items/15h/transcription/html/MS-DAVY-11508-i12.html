Vitrification

The transformation of a substance into a glass, a non-chrystelline or amorphous solid. Stones exposed to intense heat which partially melt and fuse together are vitrified.

✕

Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.
