Basalt

A dark grey to black dense to fine-grained igneous rock that consists of basic plagioclase, augite, and usually magnetite. More than 90% of volcanic rock on earth is basaltic.

✕

Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

✕

Calcareous

Composed of, or containing, lime or limestone.

✕

Whinstone

A term used to describe any hard dark-coloured rock, examples of which include igneous rocks and basalt.

Dike

A dike or dyke is a sheet of rock that is formed in a fracture of a pre-existing rock body. Dikes can be either magmatic or sedimentary in origin.
