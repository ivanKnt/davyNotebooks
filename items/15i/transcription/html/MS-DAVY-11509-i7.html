Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

Waterford

A city in County Waterford in the south-east of Ireland. It is located within the province of Munster. The city is situated at the head of Waterford Harbour. It is the oldest and the fifth most populous city in the Republic of Ireland.

JaneDavy

Daughter of Charles Kerr, an Antiguan merchant who made his fortune in highly dubious circumstances. Following his death she moved to Britain where she married Schukbrugh Apreece who died in 1807. After running a successful literary salon in Edinburgh she moved to London where she met Davy and they married on 11 April 1812 two days after he had been knighted by the Prince Regent.
