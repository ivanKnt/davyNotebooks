All the changes which have produced
our strata may have taken place
when our globe was part of another
system; but geological Theory
is of the same use in Science
That as Fable & Romance in
Morals - It releives the
dryness of discussion. It
enlivens by presenting pictures
to the imagination. & it imprints
facts more deeply in the memory
& there is no necessary
discordance between the Art of
instructing & the Art of amusing -
