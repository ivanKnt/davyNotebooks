The affections of people educated in
the country are strong & permanent
because the objects to which they
are attached are few. -
Accustomed to connect no feelings
but those of nature with things
& forms their tastes are [uncorappted]
& not at all modified by association

up ideas connected with feelings
they are not possessed of that
artificial [fondness] for [literature]

which exists amongst almost all
common place thinkers. -

amused by their own creative
