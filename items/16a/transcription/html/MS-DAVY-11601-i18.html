The attainment of the Knowledge belonging to
these highly interesting subjects is found
almost wholly upon observation; for in a
few cases only can instruments of experiment
be applied.
And the acquaintance with mineral bodies
which constitutes one of the most labourious
and most interesting of the study may in
almost every case be derived from an
examination of their external characters.
Geology perhaps more than any other departm
of Natural Philosophy is a science of
contemplation.-
