Thales

Ancient Greek pre-Socratic philosopher from Miletus in Ionia, Asia Minor.

✕

Pythagoras

Pythagoras of Samos was an ancient Ionian Greek philosopher, polymath, and the eponymous founder of Pythagoreanism. Believed in the primacy of number.

✕

Pythagoreans

Followers of Pythagoras. Pythagoreanism originated in the 6th century BCE, based on and around the teachings and beliefs held by Pythagoras and his followers.
