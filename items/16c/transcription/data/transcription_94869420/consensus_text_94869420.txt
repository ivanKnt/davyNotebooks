[underline]Geology Lecture 3 1805[/underline]
The Science of Nature depends in a
great measure upon artificial inventions for
assisting the faculties of the mind and for
increasing the activity of genius.
And it requires for its advancement a State
of society in which active patronage is
offered; and in which it is assisted no less
by the diffusion of letters than by the
application of industry, riches and power
to its numerous and [deletion]diversified[/deletion]
diversified objects.-
