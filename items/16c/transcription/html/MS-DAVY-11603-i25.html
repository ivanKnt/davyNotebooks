He proudly answered that it was not fit
for a person born to succor the whole of
Mankind to attach himself to any one
spot and that He who was destined to
read the book of Nature ought not to
have his eyes fixed upon the same leaf.
Van Helmont of Brussels like paracelsus
was a mystic but He combined much
more sound philosophy in his views of
Nature and of the Earth -
He was a diligent experimenter and a
minute observer and some of his research
researches led to important results.-
