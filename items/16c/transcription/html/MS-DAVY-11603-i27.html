The Sixteenth century was the great
era in which the mystical adept flourished.
The famous Rosicrucian Society seems
to have been established about this time
by them.
And the history of this Institution is secret
as obscure as that of the kindred Sect of
the modern illuminees.-
It would be of little use and foreign
to the objects of these lectures to enter upon
an examination of all the writings published
on Alchemy in different parts of Europe
by Valentine by Fludd by Boemen and others
