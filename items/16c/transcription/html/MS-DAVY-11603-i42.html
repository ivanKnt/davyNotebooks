In his book called subterranean philosophy
published in latin in 1669
He has given some very beautiful
details with regard to the Mineral
Kingdom and has offered views of
Theory perfectly new - his ideas
concerning combination and decomposit
are of the most accurate nature
his notion of an inflamable principle
formed the foundation of the hypothesis
of Hall - He gave to chemical philosophy
new dominions and new instruments of
investigation and He attempted to form
a general Theory in which all the
operations of Nature should be explained
