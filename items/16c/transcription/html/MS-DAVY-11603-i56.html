As a poetical Romance it is highly
interesting and entertaining but as
a philosophical opinion it is wholly
unworthy of opinion attention and it
affords one proof amongst many others
of the folly of attempts to wrest the
meaning of the sacred writings
which express general truths so as
to make them serve as supports for
hypotheses of human invention so as
to blend them with the visions
and fancies of men - _|
