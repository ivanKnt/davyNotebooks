The last system which I shall mention in
this Lecture is that of Buffon .-
|_One of the most singular and extraordinary
productions of the human mind, beauties and
defects are blended in it and it is complete
with ingenuity and with errors.
|_ Buffons idea of the primary state of the Globe
is wholly borrowed from Leibnitz. He conceives
it to have existed in igneous fusion; but despising
the modern opinion of the Author of the
Protogaea that such was its primary creation -
He supposes it to have been produced from
the ruins of another system to have been
carried off from the Sun by the powerful
impulse of a Comet and to have been moved -
