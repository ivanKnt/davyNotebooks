Plutonic Theory

Relates to the Plutonist theories of James Hutton (1715-1795), who proposed that the earth's igneous rocks were formed from magma.

✕

Neptunian Theory

A theory of geology proposed by Abraham Gottlob Werner, who proposed that rocks formed from the crystallisation of minerals in the early Earth's oceans.

✕

Neptune

The Roman god of freshwater and the sea in Roman religion.

✕

Pluto

The ruler of the underworld in ancient Greek mythology and religion.

✕

Abraham GottlobWerner

German geologist who proposed the theory of Neptunism and who also proposed the chronological sequence found in stratified rocks.

✕

RichardKirwan

Irish chemist, mineralogist and geologist who accused fellow geologist, James Hutton, of athiesm following publication of his Theory of the Earth (1788). FRSL
