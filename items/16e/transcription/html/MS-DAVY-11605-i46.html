Neptunian Theory

A theory of geology proposed by Abraham Gottlob Werner, who proposed that rocks formed from the crystallisation of minerals in the early Earth's oceans.

✕

Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

✕

Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

✕

JamesHutton

Scottish geologist, agriculturalist and chemical manufacturer. His Theory of the Earth (1795) is one of the foundations of modern geology. Davy discussed Hutton's theories in his lectures and visited some of the key geological sites that Hutton used in his work.
