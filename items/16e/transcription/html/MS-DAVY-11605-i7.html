These are rough lecture notes for a lecture copied up by an amenuensis in RI MS HD/16/F. The lecture itself was given on Thursday, 14 March 1805 and repeated on Wednesday, 5 February 1806 and Wednesday, 27 April 1808. It is published in Humphry Davy on Geology: the 1805 Lectures for the General Audience, ed. Robert Siegfried and Robert H. Dott, Jnr (University of Wisconsin Press, 1980), pp.59-72
