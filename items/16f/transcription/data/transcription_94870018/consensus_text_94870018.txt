The distinction between the primitive and
the secondary Rocks have been already
often noticed and an attention to the
difference of their nature is of the highest
importance in every part of the study of
Geology.-
All substances that contain organic remains
that bear evidence of having been produced
since the existence of living beings on
the Globe are excluded from the series
of [underline]primitive[/underline] masses.-
As are likewise all strata fused by
volcanoes [deletion]fires[/deletion] or deposited from by
[underline]the agency of Water[/underline].-
