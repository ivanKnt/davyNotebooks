On penetrating |beneath| the surface however
a new and altogether different order
of things is presented to our attention
All is dark and silent -
Soils Earthy strata and Rocks are
blended together without regularity of
form - Veins of metalic ores and of
chrystalized stones intersect them in
different directions and by superficial
examination deformity and disorder
only are perceptible in the subterranean
mineral Kingdom -
