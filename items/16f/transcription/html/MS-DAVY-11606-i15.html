The primitive Rocks are the substances
which occupy the lowest part of the surface
of the Earth penetrated by human labour.
And the form the summits of most of the
highest eminences of mountainous Countries
The great masses of primitive Rocks are of
several species which have characters peculiar
to themselves and wholly different from those
of all other substances.-
These characters it will be necessary for
us particularly to attend to
