The attempt has been made to delineate
the aspect of serpentine in this group
of Rocks No5 - Instance
Serpentine is one of the primitive
Rocks which contains the greatest number
of perforations and of orifices |caves|
And when it occurs upon a great scale
its appearances are in the highest degree
picturesque
In the large masses of serpentine which
as they exist in Nature
form the cliffs of Cape Lizard in Cornwall
nothing can exceed the variety of the
colours and the smoothness and polish of
the surface Red dark green brown and
yellow all appear sometimes distinct &
sometimes softening into each other -
