There is a similar arrangement
in other great mountain chains -
Thus in the highest eminence of
the Alps which is represented in this
sketch - The principle Rocks are
Sienite Granite and micaceous shist -
and Granite forms the great basis
of Mont Blanc -
A number of Geological descriptions
which demonstrate the same facts
may be found in writings of
Mr de Luc and of De Saussure but
it will be unnecessary for me to
multiply the instances as they are in
most cases analagous -
