All the details of M. de Saussure upon
the Alps are however in the highest
degree worthy of being perused on
account of their relation to the
structure of the Globe.-
This excellent man constantly studied Nature
with the views of a true Philosopher.-
He seldom indulged in speculation - His Theories
are few and founded wholly upon his
own observations.
And his narratives narratives are composed
with a simple and unaffected eloquence
which proves that he taught |sought| rather
to communicate knowledge than to excite
admiration.-
