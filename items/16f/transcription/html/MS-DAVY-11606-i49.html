past and the future
If Dr Hutton and Mr Werner had
contented themselves with explaining
those operations by which our Globe
is constantly preserved as a habitable
World in the series of events at present
taking place, they would have deserved
a much higher praise than that of
having been the founders of ingenious
Hypotheses
But when in attempting to explain
appearances they attribute to agents
powers which they have never been
observed to exert, or refer to effects to causes
the operation of which they are ignorant

JamesHutton

Scottish geologist, agriculturalist and chemical manufacturer. His Theory of the Earth (1795) is one of the foundations of modern geology. Davy discussed Hutton's theories in his lectures and visited some of the key geological sites that Hutton used in his work.

Abraham GottlobWerner

German geologist who proposed the theory of Neptunism and who also proposed the chronological sequence found in stratified rocks.
