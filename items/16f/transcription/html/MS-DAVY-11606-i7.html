Geology Lecture 5th

These are fair copy lecture notes written by an amenuensis (the rough notes in Davy's hand can be seen in RI MS HD/16/E). The lecture itself was given on Thursday, 14 March 1805 and repeated on Wednesday, 5 February 1806 and Wednesday, 27 April 1808. It is published in Humphry Davy on Geology: the 1805 Lectures for the General Audience, ed. Robert Siegfried and Robert H. Dott, Jnr (University of Wisconsin Press, 1980), pp.59-72
