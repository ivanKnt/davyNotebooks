Mr de Luc a part of whose theory I
noticed in a former lecture in his last
publication the letters to Blumenback
attempts to shew that the secondary Rocks
were all produced long before the creation
of Man.-
He conceives that the original Ocean
held in solution all the matters that
compose them;
And that these matters were deposited
and consolidated at the time that the
waters were peopled with marine
plants and animals.-
