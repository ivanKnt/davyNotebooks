Lead

Symbol/formula: Pb

A heavy metal that is denser than most common materials. Lead is soft and malleable, and also has a relatively low melting point.

Strontian

Symbol/formula: Sr

Old terminology for strontium, an alkaline earth metal, a soft silver-white yellowish metallic element that is highly chemically reactive. The metal forms a dark oxide layer when it is exposed to air.

Zeolite

Any member of a family of hydrated aluminosilicate minerals that contain alkali and alkaline-earth metals.
