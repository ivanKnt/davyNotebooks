Columnar basalt

Columnar basalt, like that found at the Giant's Causeway, County Antrim, Northern Ireland, is formed by the cooling process of the basalt lava flow.

✕

Zeolite

Any member of a family of hydrated aluminosilicate minerals that contain alkali and alkaline-earth metals.

✕

Quartz

Symbol/formula: SiO2

A hard, crystalline mineral composed of silica (silicon dioxide).
