Pearlspar

Symbol/formula: CaMg(CO₃)₂

An alternative name for dolomite. Dolomite is an anhydrous carbonate mineral composed of calcium magnesium carbonate.

Symbol/formula: BaCO3

Baryte

Symbol/formula: BaSO4

In modern nomenclature, a mineral consisting of barium sulphate. In Davy's terminology it might have been equivalent to baryta, BaO.
