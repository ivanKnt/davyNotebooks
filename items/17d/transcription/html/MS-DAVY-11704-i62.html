Sassafras

A type of deciduous tree known for its aromatic properties.

✕

Aqueous vapour

Also known as water vapour; the gaseous phase of water which can be produced from the evaporation or boiling of water as a liquid, or from the sublimation of ice. Water vapour is invisible to the eye due to its gaseous form.

✕

Elastic fluid

A descriptive term for a gas, emphasising its elastic quality of spontaneous expansion.

✕

Common air

The air of the lower stratum of the atmosphere, or the air that we breathe, which is a mixture of several gases, including oxygen (O2), nitrogen (N2) and carbon dioxide (CO2).

✕

Sulphur

Symbol/formula: S

Pure sulphur is a tasteless, odourless, brittle solid that is pale yellow in colour, a poor conductor of electricity, and insoluble in water.

✕

Bitumen

A sticky, black, highly viscous liquid or semi-solid form of petroleum.
