The water at the great fountain of Torre del Greco
and of the other springs in the vicinity began
to decrease some days before the eruption. -
On the 12 of June there was a violent fall of
rain and soon after the inhabitants of Reseria
situated directly over the ancient Town of
Herculaneum were sensible of a rumbling
subterraneous noise - and about 11 oClock
at night a voilent shock of an earthquake
was felt at Naples.
On the fifteenth of June soon after 10 oClock
at night an other shock occured: but not so
voilent as that of the 12th.
