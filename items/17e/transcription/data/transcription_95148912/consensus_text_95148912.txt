And I could discern the rivers of fire
flowing down from the mountain of
a bright red colour bearing upon it
great stones of a paler red -
I assure your Majesty no pen can
express how terrible it was nor could all
the Art or industry in the world have
quenched it or diverted its course.
In forty days time it had destroyed the
habitations of 27.000 persons made two
hills of a thousand paces high each
of 20 000 persons that did inhabit Catania
3000 only did remain -
