Accustomed constantly to look for simplicity
in Nature the mind can scarcely bring
it self to believe that one great
Phaenomena can be produced by
a combination of minute causes
Yet this as will be shewn often
happens in the common and general
series of Natural events. -

silently and unperceived for ages
