I accepted (says the Auther) the invitation
of the Bishop of Catania to stay a day with
him so that I might be the better able to
inform your Majesty of the extraordinary
fire which comes from Mount Etna 15 miles
distant from that City, which from the
horridness of its aspect for the vast quantity
thereof (for it is 15 miles in length and 7
in breadth) for its monstrous devastation
and quick progress may be terme an
inundation of fire a flood of fire and
glowing stones burning with such rage
