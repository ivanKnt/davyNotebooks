JohnWilliams

The son of a Welsh miner, Williams was a mining engineer with forty years' experience. He worked in various locations in Scotland; in 1789 published The Natural History of the Mineral Kingdom. Died in Italy where he was carrying out further researches.

Dysart

Scottish town in Fife. Historically, its ports enabled trading with the Low Countries; the area mined and exported salt and coal.
