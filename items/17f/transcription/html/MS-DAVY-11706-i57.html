Marl

Or marle. A loose, unconsolidated earthy deposit, consisting of clay mixed with calcium carbonate.

✕

Magnesia alba

Symbol/formula: MgCO3

Hydrated magneium carbonate; also known as mild magnesian earth. The metal magnesium (Mg) is obtained from magnesia alba.

✕

Shist

Or schist. Any metamorphic rock that can be split into thin layers because of its thin bands of micaceous minerals, with mineral grains.

✕

Iron oxide

Symbol/formula: Fe2O3

Oxide of iron. A dark red solid which occurs as the mineral haematite and as the main component of rust.
