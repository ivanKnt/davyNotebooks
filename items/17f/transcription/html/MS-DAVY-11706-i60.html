✕

Carbonic acid

Symbol/formula: CO2 or H2CO3

Now known as Carbon dioxide (CO2), or a solution of carbon dioxide in water (H2CO3).

✕

Elastic fluid

A descriptive term for a gas, emphasising its elastic quality of spontaneous expansion.

✕

Tufa

A variety of limestone formed when carbonate minerals precipitate out of water at ambient temperature.

✕

Stalactite

An icicle shaped mineral formation which hands from the ceiling - or protrudes from the sides - of caves.
