Farina

The white or blueish film or coating on the stems, leaves, and fruit of different plants.

Thomas AndrewKnight

Knight was a botanist and horticulturalist. He was the second president of the Royal Horticulture Society. Davy frequently fished on Knight's esates in the Welsh Marches. FRSL.

Alburnum

sapwood; the soft, newer wood in the trunk of a tree found between the bark and the hardened heartwood.
