He made his money from coach building which he used to pursue his interests in chemistry and mineralogy in the course of which he isolated the chemical element now called niobium. Nitrous acid

Symbol/formula: HNO2

A weak and monoprotic acid known only in solution, in the gas phase and in the form of nitrite salts.
