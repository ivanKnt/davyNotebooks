[underline]4 Manures[/underline]
No part of Agriculture is perhaps more capable
of being elucidated by chemical enquiries than
the doctrine of [underline]Manures[/underline]
The efficacy of these substances depend
upon their composition and the changes that
they undergo and their agencies upon plants
are immediate objects of Chemical science
The most important manures are those
that supply nourishment to the plant; and
the greater and the greater number of these
[underline]manures[/underline] consist of matter that had been before
organised of vegetable or animal substance
Vegetable and animal substances differ in
their composition but they contain certain
analogous principles. -
