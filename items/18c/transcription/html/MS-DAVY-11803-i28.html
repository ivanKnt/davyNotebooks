with a soil for manure It should be
employed at the commencement of that
stage of fermentation when it begins to
produce most soluble matter and most
aeriform fluid.

for the decomposition will be too quickly
lost and the process in consequence checked
and if it be suffered to remain longer
too large a quantity of organizable matter
will be carried off in the elastic products
and there will be a loss of nutritive
