WilliamMontagu

From 1788 fifth Duke of Manchester. Army officer, Ordinary Member of the Board of Agriculture and later Governor of Jamacia. Davy cited his agricultural work in Elements of Agricultural Chemistry

✕

JosephPriestley

Unitarian and political radical forced into exile in Pennsylvania. As a natural philospher and chemist who retained belief in phlogiston, he discovered oxygen and nitrous oxide though not under those names. FRSL.

✕

John BakerHolroyd

Independent-minded MP; from 1802 1st Baron Sheffield and from 1816 1st Earl of Sheffield. President of the Board of Agriculture, 1802-1806. During autumn 1803 Davy stayed with him for three weeks at his Sussex seat, Sheffield Place. FRSL.
