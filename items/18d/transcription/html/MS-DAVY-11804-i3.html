Lecture 5
All those substances that act as manures are
more or less transient in their effects. They are
either converted into organized matter in the
parts of living vegetables or they are decomposed
and changed in consequence of their Chemical
agencies. -
The least durable of the manures are
those that most immediately supply nourishment
to the plant sooner or later they are decomposed
and partily assimilated to its organs and the soil
becomes exhausted of them -
