Quintus HoratiusFlaccus

Commonly known in the English-speaking world as Horace, he was the leading Roman lyric poet during the time of Augustus

✕

Archytas

Ancient Greek mathematician, music theorist, statesman and strategist. He was a scientist and philosopher affiliated with the Pythagorean school and famous for being the reputed founder of mathematical mechanics.

✕

Empedocles

Ancient Greek philosopher whose philosophy is best known for originating the cosmogonic theory of the four classical elements.

✕

Lucretius

Roman poet and philosopher. His only known work is the materialist philosophical poem De rerum natura.
