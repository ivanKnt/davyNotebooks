Orpheus

In Greek mythology, Orpheus was a Thracian bard, legendary musician and prophet.

Hesiod

An ancient Greek poet generally thought to have been active between 750 and 650 BC, around the same time as Homer. In c. 730–700 BC. he wrote a poem, called the Theogony, describing the origins and genealogies of the Greek gods.
