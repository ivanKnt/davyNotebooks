Hiero II

The Greek tyrant of Syracuse. He was a former general of Pyrrhus of Epirus and an important figure of the First Punic War. He figures in the story of Archimedes shouting "Eureka".

✕

Archimedes

Ancient Greek mathematician, physicist, engineer, astronomer and inventor from Syracuse in Sicily.
