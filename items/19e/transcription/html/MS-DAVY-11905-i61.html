And science has given new objects and
new forms for adorning and extending
literature -
All the different branches of knowledge
have assisted each other. And like different
instruments of music, the sounds of which
combine in Harmony, They have all
cooperated in enlightening the mind in
extending its enjoyments and in exalting
the state of social Life -
Though so magnificent a structure
has been raised in science rapidly as if
by a kind of enchantment, yet it is still
unfinished and new labours and new
efforts of ingenuity are required both
