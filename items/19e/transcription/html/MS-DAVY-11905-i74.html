Xenophon

A Greek military leader, historian and philosopher, who was a student of Socrates. He recorded some of Socrates' philosophy. He is mainly remembered for his book Anabasis.

Socrates

Greek philosopher whose work survives mostly through the writings of his pupil Plato. Executed by the democratic Athenian state for sedition.
