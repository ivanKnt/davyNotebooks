Torricellian vacuum

A vacuum formed above the column of mercury in a mercury barometer

✕

EvangelistaTorrecelli

Italian natural philosopher and mathematician who invented the barometer. He was a student of Galileo.

Flint

Occasionally flintstone. A sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral quartz, categorised as the variety of chert that occurs in chalk or marly limestone.
