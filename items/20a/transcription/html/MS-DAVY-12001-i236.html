Carbonic acid

Symbol/formula: CO2 or H2CO3

Now known as Carbon dioxide (CO2), or a solution of carbon dioxide in water (H2CO3).

Flint

Occasionally flintstone. A sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral quartz, categorised as the variety of chert that occurs in chalk or marly limestone.

Fluorspar

Symbol/formula: CaF2

Fluorite (also called fluorspar) is the mineral form of calcium fluoride.

Thomas (Tom)Wedgwood

Youngest son of Josiah Wedgwood who became a close friend of Davy's. Together they published a paper which has been taken as marking the start of chemical photography.

Carl WilhelmScheele

Swedish-German pharmaceutical chemist. One of the discoverers of what would later be called oxygen (1771) as well as other chemical elements including what Davy renamed chlorine.
