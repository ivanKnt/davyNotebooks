Even to reason with accuracy concerning
the physiological effects of an agent
we must be acquainted with its
chemical properties; from these our
analogies must be drawn: & if they
they are important if they only lead
to further investigation. Here then
is the connection between the chemistry
of dead of living matter.
To ascertain what principles are
capable of altering the conditions of
life; thus without pretending to explain
the mode of their operations they
become powerful instruments in
producing pleasure & removing pain -
Analogy leads us to others - & this
is the cheif perhaps the
only use of [speculion] -
