Samuel LathamMitchill

American chemist, botanist, natural historian and botanist, who studied medicine at the University of Edinburgh. He claimed nitrous oxide was poisonous (which Davy proved was wrong).

JosephPriestley

Unitarian and political radical forced into exile in Pennsylvania. As a natural philospher and chemist who retained belief in phlogiston, he discovered oxygen and nitrous oxide though not under those names. FRSL.
