nihil est in intellectu quod not fuit prius in sensu

Nothing is in the intellect that was not first in the senses (Latin) This phrase is known as the "Peripatetic axiom"

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
