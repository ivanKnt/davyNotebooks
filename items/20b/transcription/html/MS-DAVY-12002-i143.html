Observations on the philosophy of Heat
and Light. -
- by H. Davy. -
At the time of the publication of my essays
on Light & Heat I was a very young
experimenter; from my situation
unacquainted with a number of
important works on those abstruse
subjects
Since that time my knowledge of
facts has increased & my scepticism
with regard to th & this knowledge
of facts has produced in me a
scepticism with regard to the theories
of light & Heat in general but
in spite of this scepticism the
Theory I have heretofore adopted
appears to me still the most
probable. -
