When the stomach is in
a bad state effects will
be disagreable persons may
be unpleasantly affected
& forget it afterwards
depends upon the association
of ideas, slight & momentary
excitations
April May. 5 After eating
a supper drinking a |two| glasses of
brandy & water & sitting
for some time on the top of
the wall by moonlight reading
Condorcets life of Voltaire
I requested Mr Dwyer to give
me a dose of air He gave
me six quarts which I breathed
for a long while without feeling
