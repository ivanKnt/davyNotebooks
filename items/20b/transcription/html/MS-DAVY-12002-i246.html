A Friend of mine a few days ago
requested me to examine a phaenomenon
which his son had observed. Accidentally
rubbing two common bonnet canes ( )
together He perceived a luminous
appearance. - I found all canes of
this kind to produce the same
effects The luminous appearance
was much increased by collision
when they were violently struck together
sparks nearly as bright as those
from the gunlock were produced.
I endeavored first to discover whether
the phaenomenon was electric -
The electrometer was not sensibly
affected when the canes were
rubbed together - Whenever the
luminous appearance was produced
a smell very similar to that
I have perceived from flints was
