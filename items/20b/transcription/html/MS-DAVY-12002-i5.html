Member of a family of Bristol wine merchants, visitor to the Medical Pneumatic Institution, and subscriber to various Bristol literary works. Later detained in France following the end of the Peace of Amiens.

Flint

Occasionally flintstone. A sedimentary cryptocrystalline form of the mineral quartz, categorised as the variety of chert that occurs in chalk or marly limestone.
