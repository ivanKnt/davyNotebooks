a piece of solid Carbonate of Ammonia
was inserted into a cylinder of the
capacity of 360 grains, the temperature
being 62. A hot iron was plunged
into the Mercury no gas was
liberated till the Mercury became
heated to 130°, at this temperature
gas began to be slowly given out
as was perceived at the temperature
of 200o gas was generated very
rapidly when the cylinder was
nearly full to 7 measures it was
transferred into another vessel
a diminution rapidly took &
there a white cloud was formed &
about 45 grain measures of gas
remained which was carbonic
acid. -
