AnnaBeddoes

Anna Beddoes, née Edgeworth, the wife of Thomas Beddoes who befriended Davy when he arrived in Bristol. Critics have speculated that they had an affair because Davy wrote poems to Anna and she wrote poems for him that he copied out in his notebooks.

Euclid

An ancient Greek mathematician, geometer and logician. Considered the "father of geometry", he is chiefly known for the Elements treatise, which established the foundations of geometry that largely dominated the field until the early 19th century.
