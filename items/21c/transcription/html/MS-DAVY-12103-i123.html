Lime

Symbol/formula: CaO

Commonly known as quicklime or burnt lime, calcium oxide is a widely used chemical compound. It is a white, caustic, alkaline, crystalline solid at room temperature.

Board of Agriculture

The Board of Agriculture (1793-1822) was a British voluntary association founded to promote agricultural improvement. Davy gave an annual course of lectures to the Board 1803-1812. Davy's career there was pushed by Joseph Banks.
