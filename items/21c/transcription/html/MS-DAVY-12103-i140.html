Mr Poole of Stowey
- The Theory of the operation of shell sand
is rather more complicated than that of
the marles - It contains as I said
before carbonate of Lime & animal matter
& at the same time that it improves
the composition & texture of the soil it
furnishes by the gradual action of Air &
water a food to the plant. -
- Hence its great & general efficacy
on almost all lands - It improves
though unequally. - Clays siliceous
sands - marles - & peat mosses. -
It furnishes &.
