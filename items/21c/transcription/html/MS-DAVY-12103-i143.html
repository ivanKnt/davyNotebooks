Radicle

In botany, the radicle is the first part of a seedling to emerge from the seed during the process of germination.

✕

Plumule

In botany, the primary bud of a plant embryo.

✕

Cotyledons

The first leaves produced by plants, the embryonic part of the seed that stores fuel for future growth.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
