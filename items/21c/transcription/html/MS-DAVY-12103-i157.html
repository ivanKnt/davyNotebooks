Alburnum

sapwood; the soft, newer wood in the trunk of a tree found between the bark and the hardened heartwood.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
