Thomas AndrewKnight

Knight was a botanist and horticulturalist. He was the second president of the Royal Horticulture Society. Davy frequently fished on Knight's esates in the Welsh Marches. FRSL.

John BakerHolroyd

Independent-minded MP; from 1802 1st Baron Sheffield and from 1816 1st Earl of Sheffield. President of the Board of Agriculture, 1802-1806. During autumn 1803 Davy stayed with him for three weeks at his Sussex seat, Sheffield Place. FRSL.
