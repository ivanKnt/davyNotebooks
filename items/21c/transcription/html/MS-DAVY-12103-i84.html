Iron oxide

Symbol/formula: Fe2O3

Oxide of iron. A dark red solid which occurs as the mineral haematite and as the main component of rust.

✕

Carbon

Symbol/formula: C

A non-metallic and tetravalent element. Well-known allotropes include graphite, diamond, amorphous carbon, and fullerenes.

✕

Manganese

Symbol/formula: Mn

A hard, brittle, silvery metal, often found in minerals in combination with iron. Manganese was first isolated in the 1770s.
