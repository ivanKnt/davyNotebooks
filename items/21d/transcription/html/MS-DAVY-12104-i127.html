Aqueous vapour

Also known as water vapour; the gaseous phase of water which can be produced from the evaporation or boiling of water as a liquid, or from the sublimation of ice. Water vapour is invisible to the eye due to its gaseous form.

Limestone

A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3).
