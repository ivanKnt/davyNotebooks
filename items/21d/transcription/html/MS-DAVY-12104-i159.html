Ornithorinchus Paradoxicus

Ornithorhynchus paradoxus - latin binomial suggested for the platypus in 1803 by Blumenbach, meaning "paradoxical bird-snout". Today the platypus is recognised as Ornithorhynchus anatinus meaning "bird-snouted flat-foot".
