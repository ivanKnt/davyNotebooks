ThomasBernard

Philantropist and a founding member of the Royal Institution serving as an early Treasurer. Later influential in running the institution, especially its lecture programme.

✕

ShuteBarrington

Bishop of Durham, 1791 until death. An early Proprietor of the Royal Institution who hosted Davy when he was in the North-East working on his safety lamp.

✕

SophiaBrownrigg

Wife of Sir Robert Brownrigg (q.v.), Governor of Ceylon.
