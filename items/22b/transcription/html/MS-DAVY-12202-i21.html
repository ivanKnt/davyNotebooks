Sulzer was amongst the first physicians who
discovered the power of certain metals to produce
the phaenomena of taste when applied in
a certain manner to the organs of
that sense
Galvanic noticed the stimulating agency
of such metals & other non conductors
when applied in producing muscular actions
when applied to the nerves & muscles
of frogs. To Ash we owe the
exibition of the power of certain metals
to oxidate themselves more rapidly
when in contact with other metals of
less degree of oxygenation. -
These facts & collateral ones
discovered by Humbo Volta Fowler Humbolt
Pfaff , Ritter &c formed all that was
Known of Galvanism before the
discovery of the Galvanic battery .
It is not my dessign at present to attempt
an analysis of that strange combination
nor shall I enter into any detail of
the theories that have been formed
on the subject to explain the phaenomena

LuigiGalvani

EdwardAsh

Oxford trained physician and a friend of Rumford's whom he met in Italy. An Annual Subscriber to the Royal Institution. FRSL.

AlessandroVolta

Professor of experimental physics at the University of Pavia. At the end of the eighteenth century he Invented the Voltaic pile which produced electric current and which Davy shortly dubbed the battery. Davy described meeting him in 1814 in notebook 14/I, p.38. ForMemRSL.
