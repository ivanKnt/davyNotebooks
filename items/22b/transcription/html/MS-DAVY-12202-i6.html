Zinc

Symbol/formula: Zn

A a slightly brittle metal at room temperature and has a shiny-greyish appearance when oxidation is removed.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.

✕

Tin

Symbol/formula: Sn

A silvery-coloured metal, tin is soft enough to be cut with little force, and a bar of tin can be bent by hand with little effort.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.
