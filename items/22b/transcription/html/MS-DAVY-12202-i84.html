produced by metallic contact with
water is that discovered by Dr
Ash .

Decr. 15 Attempted the expt cruc: of
the decomposition of water by means of
the two siphons & two tubes connected
by veins but without success the globules
of air carried over water with them
into the siphons -
Tried the expt with the different
substances to ascertain whether Hydrogene
& Oxygene were given out in
different proportions when different
substances were used for their products
ie on the assumption that through
Sulphuric acid &c through better conductors
than water ( might convey the hydrogen
ating influence unaltered though they
gave out much less gas than Oxygene -
A series of tubes with gold wires
was connected with a pile of 58 plates
in this manner

Hydrogene

Symbol/formula: H

Now known as hydrogen. Hydrogen is the lightest element and, at standard conditions, is a gas of diatomic molecules. It is colourless, odourless, tasteless, non-toxic, and highly combustible.

✕

Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Sulphuric acid

Symbol/formula: H2SO3

A mineral acid composed of the elements sulphur, oxygen, and hydrogen. It is a colourless, odourless, and viscous liquid that is miscible with water.
