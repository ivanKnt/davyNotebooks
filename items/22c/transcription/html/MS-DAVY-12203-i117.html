JamesHutton

Scottish geologist, agriculturalist and chemical manufacturer. His Theory of the Earth (1795) is one of the foundations of modern geology. Davy discussed Hutton's theories in his lectures and visited some of the key geological sites that Hutton used in his work.

JohnWoodward

Natural philospher with an interest in geology. His collection of fossils and minerals is part of Cambridge University's Sedgwick Museum of Geology. FRSL.

Johann JakobScheuchzer

Swiss geologist and palaeontologist within a Biblical context. He was a diluvialist and believed that rock strata and fossils were deposited during the Great Flood. His account of the supposed remains of a human who witnessed the flood (Homo Diluvii Testis) was published in Latin in 1726. ForMemRSL.
