London Institution

The London Institution was founded in 1806. Francis Baring (1740–1810), a wealthy banker, was an instigator of the London Institution and became its first President.

The Hospitals

Referring to two hospitals in Southwark, London: Guy's Hospital which was founded in 1721 by Thomas Guy, and St Thomas's Hospital which was founded in the 1100s.
