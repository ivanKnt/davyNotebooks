Puddingstone

A popular name applied to a conglomerate that consists of distinctly rounded pebbles whose colours contrast sharply with the colour of the finer-grained, often sandy, matrix or cement surrounding them

✕

Digester

A device invented by French physicist Denis Papin in 1679. It is an early form of pressure cooker. It was known as a bone digester because of its use to extract the fat from bones under the high pressure of steam.

DenisPapin
