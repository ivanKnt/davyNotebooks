The discoveries made by the
Moderns in experimental Science
have necessarily lead to more
correct & exalted methods
And since the elements of
bodies have been made known
Speculative men have been
obliged to curb their imagination
or at least shew, that
effects the same a or
analogous effects to those [xx]
which they suppose to have
taken place in Nature
are capable of being
produced by Art
