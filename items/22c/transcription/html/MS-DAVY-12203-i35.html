When Newton was asked by Dr Pemberton
to what He owed his great discoveries
He said to his habitual & patient
attention; & the same great
Man in a conversation in
his later years upon the
progress of Discovery having asked
what was doing at Cambridge
& being answed by Dr Barrow ["]that
there was nothing doing that He
had occupied all the ground to jocosely
judiciously said Beat the bushes
& there is still plenty of game
to be raised. -
- original profundity of Genius
talents for abstracted Researches
& rigorous constitution of Mind
connected with sagacity & acuteness
