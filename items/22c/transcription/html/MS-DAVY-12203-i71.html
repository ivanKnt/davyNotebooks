Oxygene

Symbol/formula: O

Now known as oxygen. Oxygen is a colourless, odourless, tasteless gas essential to living organisms.

✕

Nitrous oxide

Symbol/formula: N2O

Later known as 'laughing gas'. Davy discovered that nitrous oxide was respirable when not contaminated with nitric oxide (NO). Davy researched into the physiological effects of nitrous oxide.

✕

Hydrocarbonate

Symbol/formula: H2 + CO

Hydrogen mixed with carbon monoxide.

✕

Mercury

Symbol/formula: Hg

Also known as quicksilver. A heavy, silvery element, mercury is the only metallic element that is known to be liquid at standard temperature and pressure.

✕

aeriform

Meaning having the form or nature of air; gaseous.

WilliamHiggins

Nephew of Bryan Higgins. Professor of Chemistry at the Dublin Society where he proposed a theory of chemical atomism. Met Davy in London who supported Higgins's claim to the atomic theory over that of John Dalton. FRSL.
