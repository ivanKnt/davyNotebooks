LuigiGalvani

Italian surgeon and anatomist who carried out experiments with electricity and animals. The term 'galvanism' was named after him

Fluate of potassa

A fluate is a salt formed by the fluoric acid combined with a base; fluate of potassa is the combination of fluoric acid and potash.

✕

Potash

An alkaline substance consisting largely of impure potassium carbonate. In October 1807, Davy decomposed potash using electricity, revealing the previously unknown element potassium.
