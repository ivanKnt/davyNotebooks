The first countries in which
regular orderly political institutions
were first formed, are
obscure & imperfect; yet
when assisted by just
reasonings in physical circumstances
they will afford perhaps sufficient
light to enable us to form
general views on the subject,
or at least, they may
satisfy the imagination
without obliging it to multiply
conjecture & loose itself
in perplexity. -
