Hindoo

An archaic term, now considered derogatory, historically used as a geographical and cultural identifier for people living in the Indian subcontinent.

✕

FrancisWilford

Indologist, Orientalist, member of the Asiatic Society of Bengal, and constant contributor to its journal, Asiatic Researches, for which he wrote a number of unreliable articles on ancient Hindu geography, mythography and other subjects.
