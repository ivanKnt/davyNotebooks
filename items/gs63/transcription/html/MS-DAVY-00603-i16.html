Sandstone

A sedimentary rock consisting of sand or quartz grains cemented together, typically red, yellow, or brown in colour.

✕

Slate

Slate is a fine-grained, foliated, homogeneous, metamorphic rock derived from an original shale-type sedimentary rock composed of clay or volcanic ash through low-grade, regional metamorphism

✕

Limestone

A sedimentary rock composed mainly of calcium carbonate (CaCO3).

✕

Toadstone

Regional name for a type of igneous rock found between layers of limestone rock in Derbyshire, England. John Whitehurst wrote about it in his 1778 Enquiry into the Original State and Formation of the Earth, suggesting that the rock was igneous rather than sedimentary.
