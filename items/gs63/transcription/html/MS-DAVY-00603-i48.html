Granite

A coarse-grained intrusive igneous rock.

JamesHutton

Scottish geologist, agriculturalist and chemical manufacturer. His Theory of the Earth (1795) is one of the foundations of modern geology. Davy discussed Hutton's theories in his lectures and visited some of the key geological sites that Hutton used in his work.

Abraham GottlobWerner

German geologist who proposed the theory of Neptunism and who also proposed the chronological sequence found in stratified rocks.

✕

RichardKirwan

Irish chemist, mineralogist and geologist who accused fellow geologist, James Hutton, of athiesm following publication of his Theory of the Earth (1788). FRSL
