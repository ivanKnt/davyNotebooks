JohnPlayfair

Professor of Natural Philosophy at Edinburgh University. His Illustrations of the Huttonian Theory of the Earth (1802) gave strong support to James Hutton's theory of uniformitarianism. FRSL.

✕

JamesHutton

Scottish geologist, agriculturalist and chemical manufacturer. His Theory of the Earth (1795) is one of the foundations of modern geology. Davy discussed Hutton's theories in his lectures and visited some of the key geological sites that Hutton used in his work.

✕

Plutonic Theory

Relates to the Plutonist theories of James Hutton (1715-1795), who proposed that the earth's igneous rocks were formed from magma.
