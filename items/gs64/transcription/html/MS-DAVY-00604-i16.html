Cornu Ammonis

Meaning, the horns of Ammon, or Ammon's horns; curling ram's horns used as a symbol of the Egyptian god Ammon. Also used to describe the visually similar fossil shells of ancient snails and cephalods, which led to the latter becoming known as ammonite.
