Geysir

Hot springs located near Rykum in Iceland. John Thomas Stanley brought samples of the waters to Dr Joseph Black; Black presented a paper on his findings to the Royal Society of Edinburgh in July 1791, and the paper was published in the Transactions of the Royal Society of Edinburgh, Vol 3, pp. 95-126 in 1794.
