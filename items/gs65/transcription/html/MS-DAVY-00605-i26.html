Charcoal

A lightweight black carbon residue produced by strongly heating wood (or other animal and plant materials) in minimal oxygen to remove all water and volatile constituents.

✕

Sandstone

A sedimentary rock consisting of sand or quartz grains cemented together, typically red, yellow, or brown in colour.

✕

Reference

James Hutton (1794)Theory of the Earth.

✕

Pitcoal

Coal mined from the earth - distinguished from charcoal.
