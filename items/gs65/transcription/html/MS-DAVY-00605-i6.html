|_ None of the facts concerning
the formation of the Secondary
Rocks can be immediately
Known - All our reasonings
upon them must be inductive.
They must be founded upon
the Knowledge of the properties
of the substances that compose
the strata & of the agents
by which these substances are
capable of being modified. -
And where causes can be
plausibly develloped; it must
be from the examination
of existing operations that
produce appearances similar to those
which are the objects of [xxxx] |enquiry| -
